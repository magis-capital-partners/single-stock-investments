Item 2.02. Results of Operations and Financial Condition.

Assurant, Inc. (the “Company”) announced today that it expects its Global Housing segment to record approximately $46 million pre-tax, or $36 million after-tax, of reportable catastrophes for second quarter 2024, with no single event expected to exceed $15 million in pre-tax losses. Reportable catastrophe losses were driven by five reportable events in the quarter, primarily within lender-placed products from severe convective storm events across the Southwest, Midwest and Southeast.

The Company’s reportable catastrophes include individual catastrophic events that generate losses in excess of $5 million, pre-tax, net of reinsurance and client profit sharing adjustments and including reinstatement and other premiums.

The Company will report second quarter 2024 results on August 6, 2024.
