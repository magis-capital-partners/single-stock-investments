Item 8.01 Other Events.

On October 15, 2025, Archer Aviation Inc. (the “Company”) announced that it won a competitive bid process to acquire certain patent assets of Lilium GmbH, for a total purchase price of 18 million euros (the “Patent Acquisition”). The Patent Acquisition, which is expected to close in the fourth quarter of the Company's fiscal year ending December 31, 2025, is subject to customary closing conditions. A copy of the Company’s press release announcing the Patent Acquisition is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1.
