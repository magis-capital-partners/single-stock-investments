Item 4.  Ownership.
The information in items 1 and 5 through 11 on the cover pages (p. 2 ) on Schedule 13G is hereby incorporated by reference.
GXMC is a registered investment adviser that furnishes investment advice to the Global X Uranium ETF (the “Fund”), a separate series of Global X Funds, an investment company registered under Section 8 of the Investment Company Act of 1940. As a result of its role as investment adviser to the Fund, GXMC may be deemed to be the beneficial owner of shares of Denison Mines Corp. held by the Fund. However, GXMC does not have the right to receive any dividends from, or the proceeds from the sale of, the securities held in the Fund and disclaims any ownership associated with such rights.
