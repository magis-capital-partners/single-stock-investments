Explanatory Note:

 

This Amendment No. 9 (this “Amendment”) to the Statement of Beneficial Ownership on Schedule 13D amends the Schedule 13D filed on March 30, 2016 (as subsequently amended) (the “Schedule 13D”) by the reporting person with the Securities and Exchange Commission with respect to shares of Class A common stock (the “Common Stock”) of Centrus Energy Corp (the “Issuer”). Capitalized terms used but not defined in this Amendment have the meaning set forth in the Schedule 13D. This Amendment is being filed to amend and supplement the Schedule 13D as set forth herein. Disclosure items set forth in the Schedule 13D shall remain in effect, except to the extent expressly amended or superseded by this Amendment.
