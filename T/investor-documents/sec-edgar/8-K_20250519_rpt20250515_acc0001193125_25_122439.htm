ITEM 5.07

Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders

Annual Meeting of Stockholders

The 2025 Annual Meeting of the stockholders of AT&T Inc. was held virtually on May 15, 2025. Stockholders representing 5,478,193,265 shares, or 76.08%, of the 7,200,741,471 common shares outstanding as of the March 17, 2025, record date, attended the meeting, or were represented by proxy. Final voting results are shown below.

Each matter was determined by a majority of votes cast, except that the advisory approval of executive compensation was a non-binding proposal.

Election of Directors

The following Directors were elected by the affirmative vote of a majority of the votes cast.
