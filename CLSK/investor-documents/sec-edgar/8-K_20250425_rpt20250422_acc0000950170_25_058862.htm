Compensation Adjustment for Taylor Monnig as Chief Technology Officer

 

Following a mid-year assessment on April 22, 2025, the Compensation Committee (the “Committee”) of CleanSpark, Inc. (the “Company”) approved adjustments to the compensation of Taylor Monnig, Chief Technology Officer. Mr. Monnig’s annual base salary was increased from $410,000 to $550,000, with his bonus target increased from a maximum of 100% of his base salary to a maximum of 150%. The equity grants under the 2025 Long Term Incentive Plan (the “2025 LTIP Awards”) available to Mr. Monnig were increased as shown in the table below. The values of the LTIP RSUs shown below are based on the closing stock price of $8.77 USD as of April 22, 2025.
