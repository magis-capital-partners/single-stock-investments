## What this business is

Pinelawn Cemetery is a Long Island cemetery and memorial business based in Farmingdale, New York. **[Fact]** It is organized as a tax-exempt **501(c)(13) cemetery company** (EIN 11-1190044), not a normal SEC reporting corporation. **[Fact]** Common shares trade thinly on OTC Pink as **PLWN**. **[Fact]** FY2024 Form 990 extract shows about **$42.9 million** revenue, **$5.3 million** net income, and **$161.9 million** net assets with liabilities reported at zero on the extract. **[Fact]**
