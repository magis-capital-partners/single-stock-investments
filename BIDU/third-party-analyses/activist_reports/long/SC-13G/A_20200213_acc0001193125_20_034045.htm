As of December 31, 2019, Handsome Reward Limited, a British Virgin Islands company, was the record owner of 5,490,000 Class B Ordinary Shares, and owned 44,668 Class A ordinary shares in the brokerage account of the administrator of the issuer’s employee stock option program. Robin Yanhong Li is the sole owner and director of Handsome Reward Limited. Pursuant to Section 13(d) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, and the rules promulgated thereunder, Robin Yanhong Li may be deemed to beneficially own all of the shares held by Handsome Reward Limited.

The voting power of the shares beneficially owned by Robin Yanhong Li represented 55.4% of the total outstanding voting power of the Company as of December 31, 2019. The percentage of voting power is calculated by dividing the voting power beneficially owned by the reporting person by the voting power of all of the Company’s holders of Class A ordinary shares and Class B ordinary shares as a single class as of December 31, 2019. Each holder of Class A Shares is entitled to one vote per share and each holder of Class B Shares is entitled to ten votes per share on all matters submitted to them for a vote.

Melissa Ma, Robin Yanhong Li’s wife, was the record owner of 1,510,000 Class B Ordinary Shares as of December 31, 2019, and owned 8,477 ADSs in the brokerage account of the administrator of the issuer’s employee stock option program. She also had the right to acquire 15,232 ADSs upon the vesting of restricted share units granted under the Company’s share incentive plan within 60 days after December 31, 2019. Robin Yanhong Li may be deemed to share beneficial ownership of the shares owned by Melissa Ma. Robin Yanhong Li expressly disclaims such beneficial ownership of the shares beneficially owned by Melissa Ma.
