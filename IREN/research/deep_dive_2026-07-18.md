## What this business is
Iris Energy Limited is an Australian-incorporated, Nasdaq-listed owner-operator of renewable-powered data centers that historically earned most of its revenue from bitcoin mining and is pivoting into artificial intelligence cloud and GPU infrastructure services. **[Fact]** FY2025 revenue was **$484.6 million** (+163% versus **$184.1 million** in FY2024). **[Fact]** At June 30, 2025, the company reported installed hashrate capacity of approximately **40.1 EH/s** and about **650 MW** of operating data center capacity, with expansion underway at Childress, Texas for liquid-cooled GPU deployments. **[Fact]** FY2025 net cash from operating activities was **$245.9 million** and net income was **$86.9 million** (10-K FY2025). **[Fact]** Nine months ended March 31, 2026 revenue was **$569.8 million** versus **$313.7 million** in the prior-year period; cash and cash equivalents were **$2.21 billion**; proceeds from convertible debt were **$3.30 billion** in that nine-month window (10-Q Q3 FY2026). **[Fact]** In November 2025 the company disclosed a Microsoft GPU infrastructure contract valued at approximately **$9.7 billion** through 2031 and a related Dell GPU supply agreement of roughly **$5.8 billion** (8-K November 3, 2025). **[Fact]** In May 2026 it disclosed a **$3.4 billion** NVIDIA managed-services AI cloud contract supported by an additional **~$1.6 billion** Dell GPU purchase (8-K May 19, 2026).

## Why the market might be wrong
**Predictive attribute:** AI contract narrative versus normalized owner cash (headline **$9.7 billion** and **$3.4 billion** agreements versus filing-based mining and early AI cloud cash flow).

**[Inference]** The market may re-rate IREN as a scaled AI infrastructure landlord because of Microsoft and NVIDIA contract headlines, Texas power-site access, and peer-group migration from bitcoin miners to GPU hosts. **[Fact]** Lawrence consolidated starting owner cash is **$0.85 per share** from FY2025 operating cash flow normalization, not from undiscounted contract value. **[Fact]** Q3 FY2026 GAAP net income was **$247.8 million** in the quarter, but the filing path includes fair-value adjustments on assets held for sale, derivative marks, and impairment charges (**$188.4 million** nine months ended March 31, 2026). **[Inference]** Investors may overweight remaining performance obligation disclosures (**$9.7 billion** Microsoft, **$3.4 billion** NVIDIA per 10-Q) while underweighting **~$7.4 billion** of Dell GPU purchase obligations, convertible dilution (**$3.3 billion** of convert proceeds in nine months), and execution risk on GPU energization and customer acceptance testing.

### Moat (Munger)

Moat is **unproven**. Power-site access and operational uptime are necessary but not sufficient: bitcoin mining is commoditized, and hyperscaler customers can insource or switch GPU landlords. NVIDIA supply allocation and Dell fulfillment create supplier concentration. Until GPU services generate sustained operating profit **after** interest and depreciation, the moat remains narrative-dependent.

## Risks & inversion
**Primary risk:** Execution failure on **~$7.4 billion** of Dell GPU purchases and Microsoft acceptance testing, funded partly by convertible debt, while mining margins compress.

**Lens failure mode (levered):** If converts dilute faster than contract cash arrives, equity becomes a short-dated option on GPU energization rather than a dhando cash bond.

Additional risks:

- **Hashprice / difficulty:** Mining segment still funds pivot; network difficulty rises can erode **$0.65/sh** mining owner cash.
- **Customer concentration:** Microsoft and NVIDIA contracts dominate the growth story; termination remedies exist after cure periods (8-K Nov 2025).
- **Impairment / fair value:** **$188.4M** impairment (9M FY2026) shows asset base is subject to write-downs if plans shift.
- **Regulatory / bitcoin:** Policy changes affecting mining economics or treasury treatment.

**Inversion:** What would make this work? Horizon GPU tranches energize on schedule, service fees exceed Dell financing cost, and mining cash holds near FY2025 levels; then owner cash could approach bull overlay paths. We are not there in Lawrence base today.
