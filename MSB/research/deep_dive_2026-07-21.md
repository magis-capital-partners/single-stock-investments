## What this business is
Mesabi Trust is a passive royalty trust that receives iron ore pellet production payments from Cleveland-Cliffs' Northshore Mining operation in Silver Bay, Minnesota. **[Fact]** The Trust has no employees and no operating business; cash flows are tons shipped times contractual base and bonus royalty formulas. **[Fact]** As of April 21, 2026, there were **13,120,010** units outstanding (`MSB/investor-documents/sec-edgar/10-K_20260422_rpt20260131_acc0001104659_26_046864.htm`). **[Fact]** Fiscal 2026 declared distributions were **$16.794 million** or **$1.28 per unit** (same 10-K). **[Fact]** Calendar Q1 2026 shipments were **938,572** tons with bonus royalty **$0** because deemed pellet prices were below the **$71.70 per ton** 2026 bonus threshold (`MSB/investor-documents/sec-edgar/8-K_20260504_exhibit_msb-20260430xex99d1.htm_acc0001104659_26_054941.htm`).

## Why the market might be wrong
The market may annualize Q1 2026's zero bonus and the July **$0.05** distribution and treat Mesabi like a broken income vehicle. **[Inference]** Bonus tiers are mechanical: when deemed prices sit below the adjusted threshold, bonus drops to zero even as tonnage recovers. **[Inference]** Yield screens and K-1 complexity exclude many buyers; Horizon Kinetics holds **22.9%** while index funds pass. Proof-first component math at base case sums to about **$36.40** per unit of economic value versus a price near **$24**, suggesting the market prices trough distributions and arbitration uncertainty rather than normalized royalty duration.
