## What this business is
Mesabi Trust is a passive royalty trust that receives iron ore pellet production payments from Cleveland-Cliffs' Northshore Mining operation in Silver Bay, Minnesota. **[Fact]** The Trust has no employees and no operating business; cash flows are tons shipped times realized pricing times bonus tier formulas. **[Fact]** As of April 21, 2026, there were **13,120,010** units outstanding (`MSB/investor-documents/sec-edgar/10-K_20260422_rpt20260131_acc0001104659_26_046864.htm`). **[Fact]** Calendar Q1 2026 shipments were 938,572 tons (+105% YoY) but bonus royalty was **$0**; total Q1 royalty was $1.63 million (`MSB/investor-documents/sec-edgar/8-K_20260504_exhibit_msb-20260430xex99d1.htm_acc0001104659_26_054941.htm`). **[Fact]** Nine-month royalty income through October 31, 2025 was **$13.16 million** versus **$19.61 million** prior; cash was **$23.19 million** versus **$100.2 million** at January 31, 2025 after distributions (`MSB/investor-documents/sec-edgar/10-Q_20251212_rpt20251031_acc0001104659_25_120673.htm`).

## Why the market might be wrong
The market may annualize Q1 2026's zero bonus and treat Mesabi like a broken income vehicle, missing the Horizon Kinetics transitory-problem framing. **[Inference]** Bonus tiers are mechanical: when deemed pellet prices sit below the adjusted threshold ($71.70/ton for 2026), bonus drops to zero even as tonnage recovers. **[Inference]** Yield screens exclude royalty trusts; decades of ~9.8% forty-year total return are invisible to index buyers. Normalized distribution yield at ~$26 exceeds 8% on >$2/unit per year mid-cycle, not on depressed Q1 alone.

## Executive summary
Mesabi Trust is a contractual pellet royalty on Cliffs' Northshore mine with cyclical commodity exposure and a legal recovery option. FY2026 10-K confirms AAA arbitration commenced September 26, 2025 on idling and intercompany pricing; Horizon Kinetics holds **22.9%**. Q1 2026 bonus royalty was zero despite tonnage doubling YoY; Q4 2025 still paid $1.04 million bonus, showing mechanics intact. Nine-month royalty income fell to $13.16 million on lower pricing and a February 2025 maintenance shutdown. At $25.68 (Stooq, May 26, 2026), normalized distribution yield is **8.4%** (~$2.16/unit per year); equity-yield-curve base is **10.3%** over five years; bull **16.5%**. Dhando is partial with a strong passive-trust floor. We propose **hold** for the incumbent royalty sleeve, not watch on depressed-Q1 Lawrence math alone.
