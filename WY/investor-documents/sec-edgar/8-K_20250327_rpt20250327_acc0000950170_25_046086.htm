Section 7 – Regulation FD

Item 7.01. Regulation FD Disclosure

 

On March 27, 2025, Weyerhaeuser Company is posting presentation materials to the Investors section of its website that it expects to use in discussions with the investment community. The materials include adjustments and associated commentary to the company's previously disclosed first-quarter 2025 outlook for certain of its business segments. A copy of the presentation materials is furnished as Exhibit 99.1 to this report.

In accordance with General Instruction B.2 of Form 8-K, the information in this Current Report on Form 8-K, including Exhibit 99.1, shall not be deemed to be “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the “Exchange Act”), or otherwise subject to the liability of that section, and shall not be incorporated by reference into any registration statement or other document filed under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act, except as shall be expressly set forth by specific reference in such filing.
