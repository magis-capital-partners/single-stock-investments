Capitalized terms used herein which are not defined herein have the meanings set forth in the Original Schedule 13D.

 

Item 4. Purposes of Transactions

This Item 4 shall be deemed to amend and supplement Item 4 of the Original Schedule 13D as set forth below.

As previously disclosed, CenterPoint evaluated various strategic alternatives for its investment in the Partnership, including a sale or spin-off qualifying under Section 355 of the U.S. Internal Revenue Code, but decided not to pursue any such alternatives because they did not achieve CenterPoint’s objectives. CenterPoint may reduce its ownership of the Common Units it holds in the Partnership over time through sales in the public equity markets, or otherwise, subject to market conditions, in accordance with the applicable provisions of the Partnership Agreement. Although a transaction for all of CenterPoint’s interests in the Partnership is not viable at this time, CenterPoint may pursue such a transaction if it is viable in the future.
