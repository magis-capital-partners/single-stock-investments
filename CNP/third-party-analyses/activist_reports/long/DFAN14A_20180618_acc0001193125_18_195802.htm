Item 8.01 Other Events.

As previously disclosed, on April 21, 2018 CenterPoint Energy, Inc. (“CenterPoint Energy”) entered into an Agreement and Plan of Merger (the “Merger Agreement”), by and among CenterPoint Energy, Vectren Corporation, an Indiana corporation (“Vectren”), and Pacer Merger Sub, Inc., an Indiana corporation and wholly-owned subsidiary of CenterPoint Energy (“Merger Sub”). Pursuant to the Merger Agreement, on and subject to the terms and conditions set forth therein, Merger Sub will merge with and into Vectren (the “Merger”), with Vectren continuing as the surviving corporation in the Merger and becoming a wholly-owned subsidiary of CenterPoint Energy.

Consummation of the Merger is subject to various conditions, including receipt of all required approvals by federal regulatory commissions, receipt of orders in informational proceedings before state commissions, expiration or termination of the applicable Hart-Scott-Rodino Act waiting period and approval of the Merger by Vectren shareholders. On June 15, 2018, CenterPoint Energy and Vectren submitted their filings with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission and initiated informational proceedings with regulators in Indiana and Ohio. Further, on June 18, 2018, CenterPoint Energy and Vectren submitted their filings pursuant to the Hart-Scott-Rodino Act. CenterPoint Energy and Vectren expect to submit their filings with the Federal Communications Commission this week. Subject to receipt of the required regulatory and statutory approvals and satisfaction and/or waiver of the other closing conditions, CenterPoint Energy continues to anticipate closing the Merger in the first quarter of 2019.
