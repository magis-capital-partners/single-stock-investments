Distribution Reinvestment Plan

The Issuer has adopted the DRIP, whereby certain holders of Shares (including each of BUSI II-C, BIM, PSG and the Adviser) will have their cash distributions automatically reinvested in additional Shares of common stock unless they elect to receive their distributions in cash.
