## What this business is
Indiana Resources Limited (ASX: IDA) is an Australian mineral explorer focused on the Gawler Craton in South Australia (~5,713 km², 100% owned). The company is pre-revenue; value is driven by treasury cash after the Tanzania Ntaka Hill settlement, ongoing Loricatus arbitration over a preserved slice of proceeds, and the Minos gold program on the Lake Labyrinth Shear Zone. **[Fact]** Aug 2025 capital return of **A$0.05** per share (~**A$32.2M**) is complete; consolidated cash was **A$31.2M** at **31 Mar 2026** per the quarterly Appendix 5B.

---

## Why the market might be wrong
At **A$0.027** (Yahoo, ~4 Jun 2026), IDA still trades at roughly **56%** of Mar-2026 cash per share (~**A$0.0485**), not the extreme discount to Apr-2025 cash (~**A$0.11**) cited in the sponsor profile. The residual gap likely reflects **Loricatus litigation** (18% of net Tanzania proceeds preserved), **~A$0.9M/quarter legal spend**, exploration burn, and no maiden resource on Minos. The predictive attribute is **post-return cash floor plus legal resolution**: re-rating toward disclosed cash does not require a mine, but further distributions depend on dispute outcomes.

## Risks & inversion
**Primary risk:** Legal and tax claims on Tanzania settlement cash delay or reduce distributions, turning the cash floor into a melting ice cube.

Additional risks:

- Exploration spend without a maiden resource erodes net cash per share.
- Highly diluted option overhang (20m @ A$0.07 Feb 2026, plus smaller tranches) if the company raises equity despite profile statement of no near-term dilution.
- Sponsor-backed company profile may overweight peer comps and Minos prospectivity.
- 18% Tanzania proceeds preserved: timing and recovery uncertain until Loricatus arbitration concludes.
- Quarterly legal spend near **A$0.9M** can compress cash per share if the dispute drags.
- No maiden Minos resource: geology upside remains unpriced and unproven in filings.

**Lens failure mode (asset / event):** If the market is right that cash is encumbered or will not be returned, the equity is a call on drilling with insufficient funding, not a discount to treasury.
