Reference is hereby made to the statement on Schedule 13G filed with the SEC by Celgene Switzerland LLC (“Celgene Switzerland”) and Celgene Corporation (“Celgene”) with respect to the Common Stock of the Issuer on February 10, 2017 (the “Schedule 13G”). Under the Merger Agreement, on the Closing Date, Merger Sub merged with and into Celgene, with Celgene surviving as a direct wholly owned subsidiary of BMS.
