Item 8.01 Other Events
On December 2, 2024, Accenture plc (the “Company”) issued a news release announcing that its Board of Directors (the “Board”) has nominated Masahiko Uotani and Jennifer Nason for election to the Board at the Company’s annual general meeting of shareholders to be held on February 6, 2025. Masahiko Uotani is the chairman and chief executive officer of Shiseido. Jennifer Nason is the Global Chair, Investment Banking at J.P. Morgan. A copy of the Company’s news release is attached to this Current Report on Form 8-K as Exhibit 99.
