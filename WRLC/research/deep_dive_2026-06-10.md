## What this business is
Windrock Land Company is a Tennessee land and natural-resources company founded in 1872 (formerly The Coal Creek Company). **[Fact]** It owns and manages **over 73,000 acres** in Anderson, Campbell, Morgan, and Roane Counties in East Tennessee, with headquarters in Knoxville. **[Fact]** The company operates through two reportable segments: **Natural Resources** (timber, oil and gas royalties, carbon offset credits, communication tower and wind-power site leases) and **Recreation** (Windrock Park, the largest privately owned off-highway-vehicle park in the United States, with rental cabins, campground, general store, and **over 300 miles** of off-road trails). **[Fact]** Windrock also operates **Southeastern Forest Management**, a timber-services affiliate. **[Fact]** As of December 31, 2025, **93,298** common shares were issued and outstanding; the stock trades on OTC Pink (WRLC).

## Why the market might be wrong
The market may treat WRLC as an illiquid OTC land stub with no analyst coverage, missing a **debt-free, cash-generating recreation franchise** sitting on a large acreage block carried at historical cost. **[Inference]** At an indicative **~$520** per share versus GAAP book equity of **~$260** per share, the stock trades near **2.0×** stated book, but land and improvements on the balance sheet are only **~$10.6 million** (**~$146 per acre**) on **73,000+ acres**, while the operating business produced **~$12.9 million** of revenue and **~$4.0 million** of segment operating income in FY2025. **[Inference]** Predictive attribute: **market structure discount** (OTC Pink, ~25 shares average daily volume, no sell-side coverage) plus **family/board-controlled** capital allocation (director-related legal fees, large dividend swings). **[Inference]** Carbon offset revenue fell from **~$913 thousand** in 2024 to **~$173 thousand** in 2025, which may cause yield-oriented screens to misread normalized owner cash. **[Inference]** Any upside from fair-value land marks or royalty expansion is **not** in our Lawrence base case without filed appraisals.
