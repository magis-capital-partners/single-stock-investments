Item 4.
Ownership.
 

  (a)
Amount beneficially owned:
 
As of September 30, 2024, KKR Denali beneficially held an aggregate of 5,342,283 shares of Class A Common Stock of the Issuer based on (i) 136,794 shares of Class A Common Stock of the Issuer directly held and (ii) 5,205,489 shares of Class B Common Stock, par value $0.00003 per share (the “Class B Common Stock”), of the Issuer directly held convertible at any time at the option of the holder into 5,205,489 shares of Class A Common Stock of the Issuer, representing approximately 1.8% of the outstanding shares of Class A Common Stock calculated pursuant to Rule 13d-3 of the Act, as amended (“Rule 13d-3”). Calculations of the Class A Common Stock beneficially owned are based on 298,557,111 shares of Class A Common Stock of the Issuer outstanding as of September 30, 2024, as reported in the Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q filed by the Issuer with the Securities and Exchange Commission on November 6, 2024 (the “Form 10-Q”) plus the 5,205,489 shares of Class B Common Stock convertible at any time at the option of KKR Denali into 5,205,489 shares of Class A Common Stock, which shares will also convert automatically upon certain events specified in the Issuer’s amended and restated certificate of incorporation. Class A Common Stock has one vote per share, Class B Common Stock has 20 votes per share, and Class C common stock has no voting rights.  The common stock held by the Reporting Persons represents 1.6% of the total common stock of the Issuer outstanding, based on 298,557,111 shares of Class A Common Stock and 36,924,030 shares of Class B Common Stock of the Issuer outstanding as of September 30, 2024, as reported in the Form 10-Q.
