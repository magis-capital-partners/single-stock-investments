## Executive summary

Amazon is a **multi-engine compounder**: North America retail, International retail, AWS cloud, advertising, devices/chips, and logistics — with AWS and advertising providing the highest-margin growth engines. FY2025 net sales reached **$716.9B** (+12% YoY) with **$77.7B** net income; AWS alone generated **$128.7B** sales and **$45.6B** operating income (+20% / +15% YoY). Q1 2026 accelerated: net sales **$181.5B** (+17%), AWS **+28%** (fastest growth in 15 quarters), operating income **$23.9B**.

The investment tension is **AI capex vs. cash flow**: FY2025 capex **$131.8B** (net) crushed trailing FCF to **$11.2B** (FY-end) and **$1.2B** (Q1-26 TTM) — deliberate investment in AI infrastructure, custom silicon (Graviton/Trainium/Nitro **>$20B run rate**), and fulfillment.

**One-line view:** Dominant consumer + cloud platform with widening AI/chips/advertising profit pools; thesis intact on business quality, but **capital intensity** is the new variable for dhando sizing.

**Thesis status:** **strengthening** (fundamentals) with **FCF/capex monitoring** required.

## Munger lenses

### Circle of competence — pass (with segment discipline)

Explainable: largest e-commerce logistics platform + leading IaaS/PaaS + high-margin ads/chips overlay.

### Moat

- **Scale & cost:** Fulfillment density, purchasing power, delivery speed.
- **AWS:** Workload lock-in, breadth of services (EC2 → 200+ services per shareholder letter history).
- **Prime / ecosystem:** Recurring membership, content, devices.
- **Chips:** Graviton/Trainium/Nitro vertical integration lowers AWS COGS and creates third-party revenue.

### Inversion — what would kill the thesis?

1. **AWS growth deceleration + margin compression** from AI commoditization or hyperscaler competition.
2. **Capex overshoot** without commensurate AWS/chips returns → prolonged FCF drought.
3. **Regulatory breakup / antitrust** on retail or cloud (FTC settlement already impacted NA segment in Q3-Q4 2025).
4. **Retail margin reversion** if logistics cost inflation outruns pricing.
5. **Anthropic-like investment volatility** masking operating trends (monitor adjusted view).

### Psychology / incentives

Jassy shareholder letter emphasizes long, non-linear paths (AWS history) — aligns with patient capital allocation narrative. Watch for capex discipline vs. "AI arms race" herding (Munger lollapalooza risk).

## Strategy — AI inflection (2025 shareholder letter)

Management frames Amazon at the center of **AI, robotics, and space** inflections. AWS grew from single EC2 instance (2006) to enterprise/government standard; similar non-linear path expected for Trainium/inference and agentic AI workloads (`2025-Shareholder-Letter-Final.pdf`).

FY2025 special charges (Q4): **$1.1B** Italy tax/lawsuit, **$730M** severance, **$610M** impairments (physical stores) — adjusted OI would be **$27.4B** in Q4 vs reported **$25.0B**.
