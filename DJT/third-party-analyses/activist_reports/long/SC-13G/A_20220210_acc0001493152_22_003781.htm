Each Unit consists of one Share and one-half of one redeemable warrant, as described in more detail in the Issuer’s Prospectus filed with the SEC on September 8, 2021 (the “Prospectus”). Each whole warrant entitles the holder thereof to purchase one Share. No fractional warrants will be issued upon separation of the Units and only whole warrants will trade.
