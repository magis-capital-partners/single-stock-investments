Item 4.Ownership.

 

Item 4(a).Amount Beneficially Owned:

 

SoftBank Group Capital Ltd (“SBGC”) has delegated all of its voting and dispositive power over the Shares it holds to a three-member joint investment committee, which shall act unanimously. As of August 24, 2022 (the effective date of the Reporting Person’s appointment thereto), the joint investment committee consists of Daniel Schreiber, Shai Wininger (each, a co-founder of the Issuer), and the Reporting Person. SBGC directly holds 11,983,384 Shares. Accordingly, the Reporting Person may be deemed to be an indirect beneficial owner of the 11,983,384 Shares directly held by SBGC. Neither the filing of this Schedule 13G nor any of its contents shall be deemed to constitute an admission that the Reporting Person or any other person named herein is, for the purposes of Sections 13(d) or 13(g) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 or for any other purpose, the beneficial owner of any securities covered by this statement. The Reporting Person expressly disclaims beneficial ownership over any Shares held by SBGC that he may be deemed to beneficially own, except to the extent of his pecuniary interest therein, if any.
