Item 2.   Identity and Background.

(a) This Statement is being filed by Lawrence J. Goldstein, an
individual investor and the president and sole owner of SMP Asset

partnership ("Santa Monica Partners"). In addition, Mr. Goldstein

partnership ("Santa Monica Partners II"). Lastly, Mr. Goldstein is Chairman
of the Barbara J. and Lawrence J. Goldstein Do Good Foundation.
