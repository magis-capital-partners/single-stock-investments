Singapore Exchange Limited — FY2025 Financial Highlights (IR extract)

Source: https://investorrelations.sgx.com/static-files/5d920b13-c5bb-4280-9b84-74025f006fc5

Fiscal year ended 30 June 2025. All figures SGD unless noted.

Group results

  • Net revenue: S$1,298.2 million (+11.7% vs S$1,162.0 million FY2024)
  • Adjusted net profit attributable to shareholders: S$609.5 million (vs S$525.9 million FY2024)
  • Adjusted earnings per share: 57.0 cents (vs 49.2 cents FY2024)
  • Reported net profit attributable to equity holders: S$648.0 million
  • EBITDA: S$827.8 million; operating profit: S$743 million; operating profit margin 57.2%
  • Cash flows from operating activities: S$842 million
  • Total FY2025 dividend proposed: 37.5 cents per share (+8.7% vs 34.5 cents FY2024)
  • Shares issued: 1,072 million (treasury 3 million)

Segment net revenue (FY2025)

  • Equities – Cash: S$392.7 million (+18.7%)
  • Equities – Derivatives: S$345.9 million (+13.8%)
  • FICC: S$321.6 million (+8.6%)
  • Platform and Others: S$238.0 million (+3.0%)
