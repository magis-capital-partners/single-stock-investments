## What this business is
Japan Exchange Group (JPX) operates Japan's dominant stock exchange, clearing house, and market-data platform. **[Fact]** Brokers, issuers, and clearing members pay trading, clearing, listing, and data fees. Revenue scales with cash-equity turnover and clearing collateral balances, not proprietary trading on core operations. **[Fact]** FY2025 (Mar-2026) operating revenue was **¥198.7B** (+22.5%) with operating margin ~58.5% and ROE 23.1% (scan-tier Q4 FY2025 release; see Primary sources). Cash equities average daily turnover reached **¥7.52T** (+31.9% YoY). The group holds a regulatory and network monopoly on domestic market infrastructure.

## Why the market might be wrong
No clear mispricing signal. **[Inference]** FY2025 was exceptional: retail and inbound equity engagement drove ADV +31.9% and clearing collateral income surged. **[Inference]** FY2027 management forecast implies flat-to-down operating income vs the FY2025 peak, so the market may correctly price cycle-peak earnings at ~26× P/E. Return is franchise earnings at price unless volume normalizes and the multiple compresses further.
