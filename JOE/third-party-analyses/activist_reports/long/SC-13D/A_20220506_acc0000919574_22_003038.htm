Item 5. Interest in Securities of the Issuer.  
     

(a-e) As of the date hereof, Fairholme may be deemed to be the beneficial owner of 22,908,090 Shares (38.9%) of the Issuer, the Fund may be deemed to be the beneficial owner of 21,269,791 Shares (36.1%) of the Issuer and Mr. Berkowitz may be deemed to be the beneficial owner of 24,469,790 Shares (41.5%) of the Issuer, based upon the 58,912,504 Shares outstanding as of April 25, 2022, according to the Form 10-Q filed by the Issuer on April 27, 2022. Of the 21,269,791 Shares deemed to be beneficially owned by the Fund, 20,921,524 are owned by the Fairholme Fund and 348,267 are owned by The Fairholme Allocation Fund, each a series of the Fund.

Berkowitz has the sole power to vote or direct the vote of 1,561,700 Shares to which this filing relates.
