Explanatory Note:

 

This Amendment No. 1 (this “Amendment”) amends, in its entirety, the statement on Schedule 13G originally filed on February 12, 2016 by the Reporting Persons. On May 30, 2024, the Reporting Persons sold 26,469,422 Class A Ordinary Shares of the Issuer. As a result, this Amendment is being filed to disclose that the Reporting Persons have ceased to be the beneficial owner of more than five percent of the outstanding Class A Ordinary Shares of the Issuer. This Amendment is the final amendment to the Schedule 13G and constitutes an exit filing for the Reporting Persons.
