Explanatory Note

This Amendment No. 8 to Schedule 13D amends and supplements the information set forth in Schedule 13D filed by Apache Corporation and Apache Midstream with the United States Securities and Exchange Commission (the “SEC”) on December 10, 2018 and subsequently amended by Amendment No. 1 filed on March 11, 2019, Amendment No. 2 filed on November 10, 2021, Amendment No. 3 filed on March 15, 2022, Amendment No. 4 filed on August 18, 2022, Amendment No. 5 filed on February 21, 2023, Amendment No. 6 filed on August 18, 2023, and Amendment No. 7 filed on December 18, 2023 (as so amended, the “Schedule 13D”).

Except as set forth herein, this Amendment No. 8 does not modify any of the information previously reported in the Schedule 13D. All capitalized terms contained herein but not otherwise defined shall have the meanings ascribed to such terms in the Schedule 13D.
