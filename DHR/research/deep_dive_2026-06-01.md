## What this business is
Danaher Corporation is a life science and diagnostics platform built on the Danaher Business System (DBS). **[Fact]** Customers are biopharma manufacturers, hospitals, and labs buying instruments and high-margin consumables on installed bases (Cytiva, Beckman, Leica, Cepheid, and others). **[Fact]** Revenue comes from recurring razor/blade consumables plus periodic instrument placements. **[Fact]** FY2025 revenue was **$24.57B**; operating income **$4.69B** (~19% margin). **[Fact]** Q1 2026 revenue **$5.95B** with net income **$1.03B** (EPS **$1.45**). **[Inference]** End markets are cyclical (biopharma funding, China diagnostics), but the installed-base model produces durable owner cash flow through cycles.
