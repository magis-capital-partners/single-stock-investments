## Executive summary

Danaher is a **life sciences and diagnostics platform** company operating through the **Danaher Business System (DBS)** — lean/Kaizen culture applied to recurring-revenue, razor/blade businesses. Post-**Veralto** separation (2023), Danaher reports three segments: **Biotechnology**, **Life Sciences**, and **Diagnostics**. FY2025 revenue was **$24.6B** with operating income **$4.69B** and net income **$3.61B** (diluted EPS **$5.05**). Q1 2026 showed **re-acceleration**: revenue **$5.95B** (+~4% vs prior-year quarter implied from segment commentary) and net income **$1.03B**.

**One-line view:** High-quality **Pabrai/Munger compounder** in life science tools — durable consumables, DBS operational edge, and M&A optionality — with **cyclical biotech funding** and **China** as the main swing factors.

**Thesis status:** **intact** — fundamentals stable; diagnostics softness offset by biotech/life science tools recovery in 2025–2026.

## Archetype — Munger moat + Pabrai compounder

| Lens | Assessment |
|------|------------|
| **Munger — moat** | **Strong:** installed base of instruments + recurring consumables (Cytiva, Beckman, Leica, IDT, Aldevron, etc.); regulatory/clinical validation creates switching costs. |

## Business overview

### Segments (FY2025)

| Segment | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Biotechnology** | Bioprocessing (Cytiva), genomics tools — tied to biopharma production and R&D spend |
| **Life Sciences** | Microscopy, flow cytometry, filtration (Beckman, Leica, IDT, etc.) |
| **Diagnostics** | Clinical analyzers, consumables — hospital/lab volumes, China pressure |

**Fact** — Businesses characterized by **recurring consumables**, direct sales, and DBS continuous improvement.  
**Source:** `10-K_20260224_rpt20251231_acc0000313616_26_000062.htm` (Business section)

### Danaher Business System (DBS)

DBS is the cultural and operational moat — talent development, daily management, Kaizen, growth tools. **Inference:** DBS is the reason Danaher can integrate acquisitions without destroying returns (similar to CSU hurdle culture, but with larger deals).

### Key brands (illustrative, not exhaustive)

| Segment | Brands cited in 10-K |
|---------|----------------------|
| Biotechnology | Cytiva, Aldevron, IDT |
| Life Sciences | Beckman Coulter, Leica Microsystems, Molecular Devices, Genedata |
| Diagnostics | Beckman Coulter Dx, Cepheid, Radiometer |

### Veralto separation (Stahl spinoff lens)

**Fact** — Environmental & applied solutions businesses separated as **Veralto** (2023). Danaher is now a purer life science/diagnostics platform.  
**Inference** — Stahl spinoff framework: simplification improved transparency and capital deployment focus; remaining Danaher is **higher-quality recurring revenue** mix.
