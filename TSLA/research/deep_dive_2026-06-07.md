## What this business is
Tesla designs, manufactures, and sells battery electric vehicles, energy storage systems, and solar products. **[Fact]** FY2025 consolidated revenue was approximately **$94.8 billion**, net income **$3.8 billion**, and operating cash flow **$14.7 billion** (`investor-documents/sec-edgar/10-K_20260129_rpt20251231_acc0001628280_26_003952.htm`). **[Fact]** Capital spending was **$8.5 billion**, implying free cash flow near **$6.2 billion** or about **$1.76 per diluted share** on **3.53 billion** weighted-average diluted shares. **[Fact]** Research and development expense was **$6.4 billion** in FY2025. **[Fact]** The company reports two operating segments: **Automotive** and **Energy Generation and Storage** (10-K segment note). **[Fact]** Q1 2026 revenue was **$22.4 billion** with net income **$477 million** (`investor-documents/sec-edgar/10-Q_20260423_rpt20260331_acc0001628280_26_026673.htm`).

---

## Why the market might be wrong
The stock may price in robotaxi, Full Self-Driving subscription scale, or Optimus optionality that filings do not yet support as owner cash. **[Inference]** At roughly **$350** per share, the market may capitalize autonomy narratives while FY2025 diluted earnings per share was only **$1.08** and automotive margins face competition. **[Inference]** Energy storage is growing faster than auto but remains small relative to consolidated revenue; the market may overweight option value in the auto segment. **[Inference]** Heavy capital spending for AI training and new models may compress near-term free cash flow below the FY2025 normalized path.

---

## Executive summary
Tesla is a dual-segment electric vehicle and energy storage platform with FY2025 free cash flow near **$1.76 per share** but declining net income versus 2024. We normalize starting owner cash to **$1.75 per share** and exclude robotaxi or Full Self-Driving terminal value from the Lawrence base path. At **~$350** per share, we expect **-21.19% per year** over seven years on the base case, far below our accumulate bar. Segment overlay shows energy growing faster than auto; FSD is probability-weighted at **12%** in the option layer only. Moat **eroding**. Cycle **mid**. Dhando **none** at spot. Stance **watch**.
