## What this business is
Arthur J. Gallagher & Co. is a global insurance brokerage and risk-management consulting firm. **[Fact]** FY2025 total revenue was **$13.9 billion** (+21% versus prior year), driven by the AssuredPartners acquisition and organic commission growth. **[Fact]** Two reportable segments dominate: **Brokerage** ($8.0 billion revenue before reimbursements) and **Risk Management** ($4.2 billion). **[Fact]** The company places insurance for commercial clients, earns commissions and fees (not underwriting risk), and provides third-party claims administration and consulting. **[Fact]** FY2025 operating cash flow was **$1.93 billion**; net income was **$1.49 billion** on diluted EPS of **$5.74**. **[Inference]** AJG is a serial acquirer of local and specialty brokers, funded increasingly with debt.

---

## Why the market might be wrong
The stock trades at a premium multiple typical of quality compounders while the market may underweight **post-acquisition leverage** and **integration risk** from AssuredPartners. **[Fact]** FY2025 interest expense was **$639 million** (+68% YoY); long-term debt **$12.9 billion**; corporate cash **$1.16 billion** (down from **$14.8 billion** prior year after funding the deal). **[Inference]** Conversely, the market may underweight **cross-sell synergy** from AssuredPartners' **$3.56 billion** annualized revenue base and Gallagher's global placement network. **[Fact]** Commission revenue is largely recurring on policy renewals; **85%** recognized on effective date per FY2025 10-K. **[Inference]** No dated HK predictive attribute exists; the edge is **scale compounder at a full price**, not a catalyst with a known payoff date.

---

## Executive summary
AJG is a global insurance broker with sticky commission revenue, a long acquisition track record, and durable local-agency relationships. FY2025 free cash flow per share is about **$6.72** after estimated maintenance capex. At **~$254** (July 2026), total synthesis implies **2.29%** per year over 7 years on filing-based owner cash.
