## Executive summary

Consensus Mining & Seigniorage Corporation (CMSG, OTCQX) is a Delaware digital-asset miner formed in 2021 from Horizon Kinetics–spawned predecessors, managed under contract by Horizon affiliates, with materially all revenue from mined Bitcoin plus merge-mined Litecoin/Dogecoin. **Accounting earnings swing almost entirely from fair-value marks on cryptocurrency** under ASU 2023-08: FY2024 showed large **unrealized appreciation** income; FY2025 flipped to **unrealized depreciation**, producing a modest **reported net loss** despite roughly **$60.5M cash** and **~$31.3M fair-value digital assets** at year-end. Operating mining economics remain thin: FY2025 **operating loss ~$1.37M** on **~$4.5M mining revenue**, with hosting and depreciation the main cash costs.

**Controlled-related-party surface area is elevated:** HM Tech hosts a portion of fleets (meaningful hosting fees + loan receivable); Horizon earns a revamped fee (**1% of quarter-end crypto + equipment**, with a temporary waiver cap through May 2026); Foundry USA Pool concentrates pool economics. OTCQX liquidity and **no SEC registration** tighten disclosure familiarity costs versus exchange-listed miners.

Quality checklist: revenue/margin drivers mapped; balance sheet reviewed; governance/proxy skimmed; valuation left as framing (embedded option on BTC/network share) pending human price context.

## Inferences

- **Economic reality is bifurcated:** reported net income correlates tightly with BTC price paths through **digital-asset marks** — consistent with audited FY2024 vs FY2025 swing (`CMSG_OTCQX_Annual_Disclosure_2025-12-31.pdf`, Statements of Operations).
- **Hash-competitiveness risk is explicit:** management ties FY2025 BTC output drop not only to halving but also to **lost network share**, implying capex/competition/strategy judgments matter beyond commodity price (`CMSG_OTCQX_Annual_Disclosure_2025-12-31.pdf`, Item 15).
- **Scrypt expansion** (equipment adds, DOGE monetization per Q2 letter) shifts revenue mix toward **merge-mined alts**, partially insulating BTC subsidy decline but layering **additional token risk**.
- **Related-party rents** (hosting + fee formula) merit ongoing monitoring per Munger incentive mapping — fees are contractual but not arm’s-length market tested in footnote (`CMSG_OTCQX_Annual_Disclosure_2025-12-31.pdf`, Note 1 & Item 34).
