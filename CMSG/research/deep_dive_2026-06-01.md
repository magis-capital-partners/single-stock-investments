## What this business is
Consensus Mining & Seigniorage Corp. (CMSG, OTCQX) is a Horizon Kinetics–affiliated digital-asset miner. **[Fact]** Operating revenue is thin: **~$4.5M** FY2025 mining and hosting revenue with **~$1.4M** operating loss. **[Fact]** Economics split between commodity hash production (BTC, LTC/DOGE scrypt) and a large balance sheet: **~$60.5M** cash plus **~$31.3M** fair-value crypto at year-end 2025. **[Fact]** GAAP earnings swing on ASU 2023-08 fair-value marks (**$(2.49)M** unrealized depreciation FY2025 vs **+$15.5M** gain FY2024), not on mining cash flow alone. Interest income (**$2.42M** FY2025) is a run-rate treasury yield.

---

## Why the market might be wrong
A **dormant asset** case applies: cash plus crypto on the balance sheet may be priced below economic value while GAAP losses and OTCQX illiquidity hide the treasury. **[Inference]** At **~$30**, the stock trades below an estimated **~$42/share** liquid asset backing (cash, crypto, equipment). Hash-share loss is real; the mispricing is asset backing and optional BTC re-rating, not operating mining margins.

---

## Executive summary
CMSG is an optionality name: loss-making mining ops plus a cash-rich treasury and Horizon-related governance. Normalized owner earnings (~**$1.35/sh**) blend interest income and breakeven mining, excluding crypto marks. At **~$30** (OTCQX, ~May 26, 2026), base expected annual return at today's price (Lawrence) is **7.7%**, below the **15%** bar; bull **18.8%** requires BTC/treasury re-rating. Durable competitive advantage (Munger moat) is unproven; asymmetric payoff (Pabrai dhando) is partial via NAV floor. We propose **watch** and size for OTC liquidity; reconcile with any FRMO/Horizon sleeve overlap.
