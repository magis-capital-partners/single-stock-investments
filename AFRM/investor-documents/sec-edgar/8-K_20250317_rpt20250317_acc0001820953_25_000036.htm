Item 8.01 Other Events.
On March 17, 2025, OnePay, a fintech company majority owned by Walmart Inc. (“Walmart”), announced that it had selected Klarna Group plc, a competitor of Affirm Holdings, Inc. (the “Company”), to offer installment loan financing to customers of OnePay at Walmart beginning later this year.

As of the date of filing this Current Report on Form 8-K, Walmart continues to be an integrated merchant partner of the Company. The Company makes its pay-over-time products available to Walmart’s customers through the Company’s integrated program with Walmart, as well as through the Company’s direct-to-consumer products, the Affirm App and Affirm Card.

During the six-month period ended December 31, 2024, purchases made through the Company's integrated program with Walmart represented approximately 5% of the Company's Gross Merchandise Volume and approximately 2% of the Company's Adjusted Operating Income.1
