## What this business is
Simplex Financial Holdings Co., Ltd. (7176.T) is a Tokyo-based independent asset manager and ETF sponsor on TSE Prime. **[Fact]** It runs three lines: institutional fund management, listed ETF creation and management (Nikkei, TOPIX, leveraged, inverse, thematic, and Japan-first active ETFs), and an open-innovation layer that connects investors and banks. **[Fact]** The group traces to Simplex Asset Management (founded 1999). **[Fact]** FY2026 (year ended March 2026) consolidated revenue was **¥22.5 billion**, parent net income **¥10.6 billion**, and split-adjusted basic earnings per share **¥317.33** (`7176.T/02_Quarterly/Earnings_Releases/20260528_2026年3_月期_決算短信_日本基準_連結__finfo2026.pdf`).
