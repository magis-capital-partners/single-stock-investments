   Notice regarding stock split
 
 
 
We would like to inform you that our company has resolved to split its stock at the board of directors meeting held today. 
 
Note
 
 
1. Purpose of stock split
With a view to expanding the shareholder structure in the future, we will reduce the amount per investment unit of our stock.
The aim is to attract investment. 
 
2. Overview of stock split
(1) Method of division
With Saturday, September 30, 2017 as the record date, the shareholders listed or recorded in the final shareholder register on that date
The Company's common stock will be split into 10 shares per share. 
 
(2) Number of shares increased due to split
 
① Total number of issued shares before stock split: 214,800 shares
② Number of shares increased due to this split: 1,933,200 shares
③ Total number of outstanding shares after stock split: 2,148,000 shares
④ Total number of authorized shares after stock split: 12,000,000 shares
 
(3) Schedule of division
 
① Publication date of record date: Tuesday, September 12, 2017
② Base date: Saturday, September 30, 2017
③ Effective date: Sunday, October 1, 2017
 
(4) Others
There will be no change in the amount of capital as a result of this stock split.
