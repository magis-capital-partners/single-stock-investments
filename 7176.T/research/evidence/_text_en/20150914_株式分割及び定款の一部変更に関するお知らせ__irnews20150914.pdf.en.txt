   Notice regarding stock split and partial changes to the articles of incorporation
 
 
 
At our board of directors meeting held today, we have resolved to split our stock and partially amend our articles of incorporation.
I will let you know. 
Note
1. Purpose of stock split
The purpose is to lower the amount per investment unit of our company's shares to improve liquidity and expand the investor base.
We are doing so. 
 
2. Overview of stock split
(1) Method of division
With Wednesday, September 30, 2015 as the record date, the stock held by shareholders listed or recorded in the final shareholder register on the same day.
The Company's common stock will be split into 10 shares per share. 
 
(2) Number of shares increased due to split
 
① Total number of outstanding shares before stock split: 147,000 shares
② Number of shares increased due to this split: 1,323,000 shares
③ Total number of outstanding shares after stock split: 1,470,000 shares
④ Total number of authorized shares after stock split: 12,000,000 shares
 
(3) Schedule of division
 
① Publication date of reference date: Monday, September 14, 2015
② Base date: Wednesday, September 30, 2015
③ Effective date: Thursday, October 1, 2015
 
(4) Others
There will be no change in the amount of capital as a result of this stock split.
