We would like to inform you that our company has resolved to split its stock at the board of directors meeting held today.

1. Purpose of stock split
The purpose of this project is to create an environment that enables the acquisition and cancellation of treasury stock as a measure to return profits to shareholders.
I will. Regarding the expansion of our investor base, which we have been considering up until now, we have put the consideration of specific measures on hold for now.
