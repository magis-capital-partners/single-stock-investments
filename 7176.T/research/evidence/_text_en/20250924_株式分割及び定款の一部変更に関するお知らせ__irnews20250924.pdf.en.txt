   Notice regarding stock split and partial changes to the articles of incorporation
 
 
 
At our board of directors meeting held today, we have resolved to split our stock and partially amend our articles of incorporation.
I will let you know. 
Note
1. Purpose of stock split
Considering the current situation where the investment unit of our stock is high, we will comprehensively evaluate the liquidity of the stock and the ease of trading in the market.
The purpose of this stock split is to maintain an appropriate level of investment units, taking into account the current situation. 
 
2. Overview of stock split
(1) Method of division
With Friday, October 31, 2020 as the record date, the shareholders listed or recorded in the final shareholder register on that date
The Company's common stock will be split into 20 shares for each share.
