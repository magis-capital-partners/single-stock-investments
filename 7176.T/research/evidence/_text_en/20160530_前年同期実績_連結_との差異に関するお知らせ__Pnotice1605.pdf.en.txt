2. Reason for the difference
The Group's assets under management at the end of the current fiscal year were 562.2 billion yen, an increase of 52.6 billion yen from the end of the previous fiscal year.
This has decreased. Basic fees based on the balance of assets under management have increased, but performance fees (according to the performance of the fund) have increased.
Operating revenue decreased 27.9% to 5,532 million yen due to a significant decrease in compensation (remuneration). 
Operating expenses and general and administrative expenses decreased by 15.4% compared to the end of the previous fiscal year in an effort to further reduce expenses.
However, as a result, both operating income and ordinary income decreased by over 40% compared to the same period last year.
