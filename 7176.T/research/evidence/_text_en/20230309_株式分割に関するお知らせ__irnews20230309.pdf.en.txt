   Notice regarding stock split
 
We would like to inform you that our company has resolved to split its stock at the board of directors meeting held today. 
 
Note
 
1. Purpose of stock split
Our stock price increased due to trading at a time when the liquidity of TOKYO PRO Market, where our company is listed, was extremely low.
Therefore, the Company's stock price currently exceeds the desirable investment unit recommended by the TSE, and therefore we are not considering lowering it.
It is the purpose. Additionally, it will be possible to acquire and cancel treasury stock as a measure to return profits to shareholders.
Our aim is to create a safe environment. 
Regarding expansion of the investor base, pending consideration of specific measures, please refer to TOKYO PRO, where our company is listed.
We believe that this will be difficult given the liquidity of the market. Therefore, we are withholding specific consideration at this time.
It's the situation. 
 
2. Overview of stock split
(1) Method of division
With Friday, March 31, 2023 as the record date, owned by shareholders listed or recorded in the final shareholder register on that date.
The Company's common stock will be split into 10 shares per share. 
 
(2) Number of shares increased due to split
 
① Total number of outstanding shares before stock split: 520,000 shares
② Number of shares increased due to this split: 4,680,000 shares
③ Total number of outstanding shares after stock split: 5,200,000 shares
④ Total number of authorized shares after stock split: 12,000,000 shares
 
(3) Schedule of division
 
① Publication date of record date: Thursday, March 9, 2023
② Reference date: Friday, March 31, 2023
③ Effective date: Saturday, April 1, 2023
 
(4) Others
There will be no change in the amount of capital as a result of this stock split.
