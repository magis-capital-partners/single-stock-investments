I Notes on significant accounting policies
1. Securities evaluation standards and methods
Affiliated company stocks
Stated at cost based on the moving average method. 
 
Other securities
things with market value
The market value method is based on the market price as of the end of the fiscal year (all valuation differences are calculated directly into net assets).
(The cost of sales is calculated using the moving average method.)
Things with no market price
Cost method using moving average method
 
2. Criteria for converting significant foreign currency-denominated assets or liabilities into Japanese currency
Monetary receivables and payables denominated in foreign currencies are converted into yen using the spot exchange rate at the end of the fiscal year.
The difference is treated as profit or loss. Also, foreign currency-denominated securities (other securities)
is converted into yen using the spot exchange rate at the end of the fiscal year, and the exchange difference is included in net assets.
It is included in valuation difference on available-for-sale securities.
