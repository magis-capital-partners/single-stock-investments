2. Reason for the difference
The Group's assets under management at the end of the current interim consolidated accounting period were 509.2 billion yen, an increase of 53.0 billion yen from the end of the previous consolidated fiscal year.
This resulted in a decrease in the yen. Basic fees based on the balance of assets under management decreased only slightly, but performance fees (based on the performance of the fund)
Operating revenue decreased 28.4% from the same period of the previous year to 2,101 million yen due to a significant decrease in compensation (remuneration recorded as such). 
Operating expenses and general and administrative expenses decreased by 9.7% year on year due to efforts to further reduce expenses, but as a result, operating income decreased.
Both profit and ordinary profit decreased by over 40% compared to the same period last year.
