## What this business is
Snowflake runs a cloud data platform: customers store and analyze data on consumption-based contracts. **[Fact]** Product revenue (usage of the platform) drives economics; professional services are small. **[Fact]** FY2025 total revenue was **$3.63 billion** (+29% year-over-year); product revenue **$3.46 billion** (+30%). **[Fact]** FY2025 free cash flow was **$884 million** on **~334 million** shares (~**$2.65** per share). **[Fact]** Q1 fiscal 2027 (ended April 30, 2026) revenue was **$1.39 billion** (+33% year-over-year) per the earnings release. The company is positioning the platform as the **AI Data Cloud** (Cortex, Snowflake Intelligence).

## Executive summary
Snowflake is a consumption-based data platform with strong product revenue growth and improving free cash flow, now explicitly marketed around AI workloads. FY2025 filings show **$3.63 billion** revenue and **$884 million** free cash flow; Q1 FY27 accelerated to **$1.39 billion** revenue (up 33 percent year-over-year). At about **$240** per share (post-earnings, May 2026), we expect **6.7%** per year over ten years on **$2.65** starting free cash flow per share, below our about-fifteen-percent target. Asymmetric payoff (Pabrai dhando) is **partial**: free cash flow is positive, but the stock is volatile and moat is still **unproven**. Marvin proposes **watch** until either price normalizes or owner cash per share proves out at the new run rate. AI sensitivity (normalized **$4** free cash flow per share) implies about **14.9%** per year (not the stance gate).
