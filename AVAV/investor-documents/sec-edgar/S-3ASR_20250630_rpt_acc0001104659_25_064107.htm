Certain Anti-Takeover Provisions of Delaware Law, Our Certificate of Incorporation and Our Bylaws
Some provisions of the Delaware General Corporation Law (“DGCL”) and our Certificate of Incorporation and Bylaws, contain provisions that could make certain transactions more difficult, including an acquisition of us by means of a tender offer, an acquisition of us by means of a proxy contest or otherwise, or the removal of any of our incumbent officers and directors.
