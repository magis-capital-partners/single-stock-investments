## What this business is
Aehr Test Systems (NASDAQ: AEHR) designs, manufactures, and sells wafer-level burn-in and test systems, led by the FOX-XP multi-wafer test platform and WaferPak contactors. **[Fact]** FY2025 consolidated revenue was **$59.0 million**, down from **$66.2 million** in FY2024 and below the **$65.0 million** FY2023 level that followed the SiC capacity build-out. **[Fact]** FY2025 net loss was **$3.9 million** after net income peaked at **$33.2 million** in FY2024. **[Fact]** The company reported **$36.9 million** in cash and no term debt as of February 27, 2026, with roughly **31.5 million** diluted shares outstanding. **[Inference]** Owner cash is cyclically depressed after a major customer paused orders; the equity trades as a SiC and AI burn-in recovery option at a price far above normalized component value.

---

## Why the market might be wrong
The market may be pricing **SiC wafer-level burn-in and AI accelerator test recovery** while underweighting **FY2025 operating cash outflow of $7.4 million**, **customer concentration risk** disclosed in the 10-K, and **dilution overhang** from the April 2026 **$60 million** at-the-market equity program. **[Inference]** Nine-month FY2026 net income of **$8.5 million** shows partial recovery, but Q3 revenue of **$10.3 million** was down year over year; normalized mid-cycle owner cash near **$0.16 per share** supports a partial balance-sheet floor, not a high-teens annual return at **$77.36**.
