## Company

Private Arizona landowner (since 1885) and lessor of grazing, renewable energy (solar/wind), and mineral acreage. Related entities include Aztec Land Company, LLC (stapled units), Apache Railway, and Aztec Minerals, LLC. Not an SEC registrant; audited tax-basis annual reports filed on OTC Markets.
