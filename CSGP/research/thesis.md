## One-line thesis

Wide-moat CRE data and marketplace subscriptions fund an expensive Homes.com and Matterport expansion — moat intact in core; GAAP earnings not yet proof of compounder status.

## Conviction drivers

- CoStar Suite / LoopNet switching costs and data depth
- ~89% revenue from ≥1-year subscriptions
- FY2025: $3.25B revenue, 79% gross margin, $480M adj. EBITDA
- Q1 2026: $897M revenue, $161M adj. EBITDA

## Key risks

- Homes.com marketing burn and competitive share battle
- Matterport/Domain integration and amortization (GAAP NI $7M FY2025)
- Debt ~$1B+ non-current post-deals
- CRE cycle slowdown on new logos
