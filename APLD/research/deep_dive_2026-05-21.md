## Executive summary

Applied Digital is a **high-power AI data centre developer/operator** transitioning from crypto-hosting roots to **HPC/AI colocation** under long-term leases with CoreWeave and an investment-grade hyperscaler. Fiscal Q3 2026 (ended Feb-28-2026) showed **$126.6M revenue** (+139% YoY) and **$44.1M adjusted EBITDA**, but GAAP **net loss to common of $100.9M** due to Cloud Services held-for-sale accounting, stock-based comp, and buildout costs.

Contracted capacity exceeds **600 MW** across Polaris Forge 1 (400 MW CoreWeave), Polaris Forge 2 (200 MW hyperscaler), and Delta Forge 1 (300 MW IT load, ground broken Jan-2026). Subsequent to quarter: **$2.15B** 6.750% senior secured notes for Polaris Forge 2 development.

**One-line view:** Operational inflection and lease-backed revenue visibility are **strengthening** the business case, but **capital structure complexity, customer concentration, and GAAP losses** keep this outside classic Pabrai dhando until balance sheet and Cloud Services exit clarify.

**Thesis status:** **strengthening** (operations/leases) with **capital-structure risk** (medium-term).

## Business overview

Applied Digital designs, builds, and operates **high-performance, sustainably engineered data centres** for AI/HPC workloads. Two reportable segments (pre held-for-sale):

1. **Data Center Hosting Business** — Colocation/hosting (Jamestown 106 MW, Ellendale 180 MW at full capacity as of Feb-26).
2. **HPC Hosting Business** — Purpose-built AI factories (Polaris Forge 1/2, Delta Forge 1); **$71.0M / $182.3M** revenue in Q3 / 9M FY26 from this segment.
3. **Cloud Services Business** — Classified **held for sale** (strategic shift); excluded from adjusted metrics.

## Munger lenses

### Circle of competence — borderline

Business model is understandable (build DC → long-term lease → collect rent). **Financing stack** (multiple SPVs, preferred, warrants, notes) pushes outside simple Pabrai territory.

### Moat

- **Site + power access:** Multi-hundred-MW campuses in ND and southern US.
- **Execution track record:** First 100 MW HPC DC operational at Polaris Forge 1.
- **Weak moat elements:** Commoditizing industry; customer has alternatives; no proprietary technology moat.

### Inversion — what would kill the thesis?

1. **CoreWeave or hyperscaler default/renegotiation** → concentrated cash flows at risk.
2. **Construction delays / cost overruns** on 150 MW Bldg 3 or Polaris Forge 2.
3. **Refinancing failure** on stacked secured debt ($2.15B notes, 9.25% 2030 notes, SMBC loan covenants).
4. **Cloud Services sale fails** or closes at distressed value — GAAP noise persists.
5. **AI capex cycle downturn** → lease-up risk on Delta Forge 1.

### Psychology / incentives

Co-founder Jason Zhang appointed **President** (Apr-2026) to scale AI Factory platform — positive for execution focus. Watch incentive alignment via stock-based comp (**$48.9M** in Q3 per 10-Q reconciliation).
