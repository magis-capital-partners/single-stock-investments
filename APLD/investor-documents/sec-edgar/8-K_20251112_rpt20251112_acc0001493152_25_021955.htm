Item 8.01 Other Events.

 

Press Release

 

On November 12, 2025, Applied Digital Corporation (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing anticipated additional equity financing to support the continued build-out of its Polaris Forge 1 and Polaris Forge 2 AI Factory campuses, as well as an additional $65 million in financing through a revolving credit agreement with First National Bank of Omaha. A copy of the press release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein by reference.
