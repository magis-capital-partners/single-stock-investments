# Cadiz Inc. (CDZI)

**Ticker:** CDZI | **Exchange:** NASDAQ | **Market:** US  
**CIK:** 0000727273 | **Last updated:** 2026-06-11

California water and land company developing the **Mojave Groundwater Bank** (San Bernardino County). Owns ~46,000 acres, permitted groundwater supply/storage, and a 220-mile pipeline slated for water conversion. Operating subsidiary **ATEC** sells groundwater filtration equipment.
