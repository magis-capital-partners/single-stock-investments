Item 8.01 Other Events.

On May 28, 2025, CBRE Services, Inc., a Delaware corporation and wholly-owned subsidiary of the Company (“Services”), redeemed all of its outstanding $600 million aggregate principal amount of 4.875% senior notes due 2026 (the “Notes”) in accordance with the provisions of the Notes and the indenture governing the Notes. As such, the obligations of Services and the Company under the Notes and the indenture with respect to such Notes were discharged on that date.
