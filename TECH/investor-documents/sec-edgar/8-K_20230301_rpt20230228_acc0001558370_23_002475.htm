Item 8.01 Other Events

​

On February 28, 2023, Bio-Techne Corporation (the “Company”) determined that Wilson Wolf Manufacturing LLC, (“Wilson Wolf”) had met the trailing twelve month earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization (“EBITDA”) target as set forth in the Investment and Acquisition Option Agreement between the parties dated December 13, 2021, as amended. Bio-Techne has further decided to exercise its option to acquire an approximately 20% investment interest in Wilson Wolf. A copy of the press release issued by Bio-Techne Corporation on March 1, 2023 announcing its intention to exercise the option to invest in Wilson Wolf is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1.
