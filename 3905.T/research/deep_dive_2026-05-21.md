## Executive summary

DataSection (**TSE Growth 3905**; filings as *Datasection Inc.*) has repositioned around **GPU-centric AI infrastructure** (“TAIZA” stack, hyperscale-style projects Japan/Australia/Thailand/UAE/Europe/US pipeline) after a long tail of retail / marketing-tech and SI businesses. Recognition of hyperscaler-scale **AI Infrastructure revenue began September 2025**; consolidated **nine-month net sales were ¥15,929 million** (**+673% YoY**) with **Adjusted EBITDA ¥698 million positive** versus **¥(49) million** prior-year period, while **reported operating loss remained ¥332 million** (stock comp, amortisation bridge). Balance sheet exploded to **¥22.3 bn total assets** (from **¥4.6 bn** at FY2025) with material **GPU capex commitments** and vendor financing archetype (deposits, subscription rights).

**April 2026 forecast revision:** full-year FY2026 (Mar) **net sales lowered to ¥33,601 million** (prior ¥37,273m) chiefly due to **slipped DC/GPU deliveries** shifting revenue **into FY2027**, partially offset by **stronger incumbent demand** from the anchor customer arrangement; reported **profit lines were revised upward** (e.g., **Operating profit ¥3,635m**, **Net profit attributable to parent ¥2,804m**, **EPS ¥115.57**) aided by non-recognition of **¥818m** equity compensation on waived 22nd-series options — so **adjusted EBITDA mechanically fell** (**¥4,252m**) as parallel depreciation/step-in assumptions shifted.

Independently, auditors’ review language in the quarterly package flags **persistent operating losses**, **capital intensity**, **very large incremental financing / dilution** (23rd series rights to **FPF**) and acknowledges **explicit going-concern discussion** formally mitigated by liquidity plans → this is **not** a tidy compounder dossier yet; payoff is conditioned on flawless execution versus NVIDIA supply, partner assembly, geopolitical shipment timing, and **customer / funding concentration**.

**Primary genius lenses (portfolio index):** *Munger* (circle of competence, inversion, incentives around financing & options) plus *Pabrai* (reward must dwarf “tails I lose badly” capex/obsolescence risk). Not a Stahl croupier; treat as transformational operating business with asymmetric **upside iff** infra contracts and GPU supply hold.

## Munger · Pabrai · (non-)Stahl application

**Munger — inversion:** Ask what zeroes equity: catastrophic **GPU sourcing failure**, hyperscaler churn, liquidity freeze without warrant exercise → each shows up plainly in disclosures; going-concern stress test is textual (`en-ir-20260216001.pdf` pp.7–8). **Psychology:** avoid **lollapalooza optimism** marrying AI TAM citations (macro paragraphs p.4) with idiosyncratic infra bottlenecks (April revision).

**Stahl lens:** Explicitly **not** exchange/passive infra tolling; avoids Stahl-framed valuation shortcuts used for ICE/8697.T/TMX-type names (`_system/reference/investment-wisdom/INDEX.md`).
