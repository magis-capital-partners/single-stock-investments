## Executive summary

Datasection remains in a **high-velocity pivot** to **AI infrastructure (“Neo Cloud”)**: GPU capacity, proprietary cluster stack **TAIZA**, and owned/operated AI data centres (Japan, Australia, and a disclosed multi-project pipeline abroad). FY2025 consolidated revenue was **¥2,942 million**; the **revised** FY2026 (Mar-26) forecast is **¥33,601 million** revenue, **¥3,635 million** operating profit, and **¥2,804 million** parent net profit (EPS **¥115.57**), with **adjusted EBITDA** revised to **¥4,252 million** (details and bridge in `en-ir-20260421001.pdf`).

Between the 2026-05-21 workspace review and this note, **primary English disclosures added material execution and financing detail**: the first Japan AI data centre was **relocated from Osaka to Inzai, Chiba**, for **latency** at the request of a **“one of the world’s largest cloud service providers”** customer; **GPU plans shifted from B200 toward B300** (with **Compal** for **635 servers / 5,080 B300 GPUs**, **USD 325 million**); **phased operations** are targeted **May–July 2026**; the prior **Giga Computing** B200 order (**48 units taken; 577 units unacquired**) is **under discussion**; financing is described as a mix of **customer advances (under discussion)**, **borrowings (~¥30.6B indicated)**, and **23rd-series stock acquisition rights exercise (~¥6.7B)** (`en-ir-20260507001.pdf`).

**One-line view:** Transformational AI-infrastructure story with a **documented revenue inflection** and **upgraded compute roadmap**, offset by **heavy capex, contingent vendor exposure, and reliance on partner + unnamed hyperscaler economics** — sizing should stay **execution- and balance-sheet-first**.

### Revision drivers (Apr-2026 forecast vs Jan-2026 prior forecast)

- **Revenue down ~9.9%** vs prior forecast due to **delayed DC operations** (Japan + Australia phase 1) while **additional services to the same customer exceeded initial assumptions** (`en-ir-20260421001.pdf`).
- **Operating / ordinary / net profit revised up** partly because **¥818 million share-based comp** from **22nd SAR** will **not** be recognised after **full acquisition at no cost and cancellation**; **adjusted EBITDA down** partly because **¥2,304 million depreciation** on initial Japan/Australia DC **not recognised** in FY2026 timing per same filing narrative.

Source: `3905.T/IR/en_financial_report/en-ir-20260421001.pdf` (pp. 2–3 of extraction).

### May-2026 GPU / site update (new since prior deep dive)

- **Site change:** first Japan AI DC moved from **Osaka** to **Inzai City, Chiba** for **customer latency** reasons; **fit-out substantially complete**; **phased operations May–July 2026**.
- **Compute change:** customer requested **upgrade to B300**; **Compal** agreement for **635** B300 servers (**5,080 GPUs**), **USD 325 million** (**~¥50,935 million** at disclosed FX).
- **Giga B200 order:** **48 units acquired**; **577 unacquired — discussions ongoing**; **no AP recognised** for undelivered portion; outstanding **unpaid contractual balance USD 252 million (~¥39,994 million at Mar-31-2026 rate)** noted with **no purchaser penalties** for delayed payment in agreement description.
- **Financing (indicative):** customer advances **¥13,625 million**, borrowings **¥30,561 million**, **23rd SAR** exercise proceeds **¥6,748 million**; **reasonable prospect** of loans but **timing gaps** may **increase temporary borrowings**.
- **Outlook:** impact to be reflected in **FY2027** forecast in connection with **“Summary of Consolidated Financial Results for the Fiscal Year Ended 31 March 2026” scheduled 15 May 2026** — *that filing is not present in this workspace snapshot as of this run*.

Source: `3905.T/IR/en_financial_report/en-ir-20260507001.pdf` (full notice); PR variant `en-ir-20260507002.pdf`.

## Inferences

- **Revenue quality** in the AI infra ramp is likely **tied to a very small set of customers and partners**; **concentration risk** is elevated even if names are redacted in English materials.
- **Margin path** in FY2026 is sensitive to **commissioning timing**, **depreciation start**, and **how much revenue is pass-through server/DC cost** vs **owned capacity economics** (9M shows large “server usage fees”).
- **Balance sheet / liquidity** thesis must include **GPU vendor discussions (Giga)** and **lender conditions**; the May notice is explicit about **financing dependency** and **amendments under negotiation** with the customer.

- **Valuation** cannot be anchored confidently on **FY2026 EPS** until **FY2026 actuals** and **FY2027 guidance** incorporate the **Compal B300** programme and any **Giga true-up**.
