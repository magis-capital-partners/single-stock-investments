## Executive summary

DataSection continues its **Neo Cloud / AI infrastructure** pivot—GPU-heavy data centre capacity, TAIZA stack, hyperscaler-linked services—with a **dramatic FY2026 revenue ramp** still guided at **¥33.6B** (vs FY2025 actual **¥2,942M**) per the **16 April 2026** forecast revision (`3905.T/IR/en_financial_report/en-ir-20260416003.pdf`).

Since the **2026-05-21** deep dive, **7 May 2026** disclosures add material detail:

- Japan’s **first AI data centre site moved from Osaka to Inzai, Chiba** for latency; fit-out **substantially complete**; phased operations **May–July 2026** (`3905.T/IR/en_financial_report/en-ir-20260507001.pdf`, §1).
- **635 servers / 5,080 NVIDIA B300 GPUs** secured via **Compal** (~**USD 325M**, ~**JPY 50,935M** at USD 1 = JPY 156.57 end-Apr 2026) replacing the planned **B200** footprint for this project; transaction terms **under amendment** (`en-ir-20260507001.pdf`; press `en-ir-20260507002.pdf`).
- **Financing bridge disclosed:** expected **advance from Customer JPY 13,625M**, **borrowings JPY 30,561M**, **23rd series stock acquisition rights JPY 6,748M**—with caveat that borrowing may spike if timings slip (`en-ir-20260507001.pdf`, p. 4).
- **Overhang:** **577 un-acquired B200-category units** remain under discussion with **Giga Computing**; **USD 252M** (~JPY **39,994M**) unpaid but **no AP recognised** absent delivery (**31 Mar 2026** rate JPY **158.71**/USD); **48 units** already acquired to be redeployed to other DC projects (`en-ir-20260507001.pdf`, pp. 2–3).

**Strategy comms:** **24 March 2026** PR targets scaling to **100,000–300,000 GPUs by end‑2026** (aspirational; not a contractual commitment) (`3905.T/IR/en_financial_report/en-ir-20260324001.pdf`).

**Japanese interim primary source:** **Semi-annual securities report** (EDINET) for **26th fiscal period interim** (1 Apr 2025 – 30 Sep 2025), filed **14 Nov 2025**—**interim revenue ¥1,746M** (+28.5% YoY), **operating loss ¥1,370M**, **parent interim net loss ¥1,465M**; notes **AI DC services from Sep 2025** (`3905.T/IR/yuho_pdf/S100X4Q7/00.pdf`, MD&A excerpts).

## Inferences

- **Upgrade economics:** B300 substitution likely increases **capex and/or lease intensity** vs B200 plan; **“parties discussing amendments”** to commercial terms implies **revenue/margin profile may differ** from models built on July 2025 framing.
- **Liquidity path is specified but timing-sensitive:** bridge financing **explicitly** ties to customer advances and **23rd** rights exercise; slippage **increases interim debt** (disclosure language).
- **Contingent liability / execution risk** on **Giga** stub (577 units, USD 252M unpaid) — resolution could mean **additional cash, penalties, or redeployment**; accounting note (no AP until delivery) is **not** the same as **no commercial risk**.
- **Scale target (100k–300k GPUs by YE2026)** is **IR ambition**; treat as **sensitivity / marketing** unless backed by orders and capital plan in audited materials.
