## What this business is

Strategy Inc. (NASDAQ: MSTR; formerly MicroStrategy) is a public bitcoin treasury vehicle wrapped around a legacy enterprise analytics software franchise. **[Fact]** FY2025 revenue was **$463.5 million**, mostly subscription and support for Strategy One / cloud analytics. **[Fact]** The balance sheet is dominated by bitcoin: **672,500 BTC** at fair value **$58.9 billion** at December 31, 2025, rising to **762,099 BTC** (**$64.0 billion** fair value) at March 31, 2026 per the Q1 10-Q. **[Fact]** The company funds purchases with convertible notes, preferred stock (STRK, STRF, STRD, STRC), and common equity ATM programs; long-term debt was **~$8.2 billion** and temporary equity (preferred) **~$9.0 billion** at Q1 2026. Operating cash flow from software was **$67.2 million** in FY2025 while GAAP net income swung on **$5.4 billion** of unrealized BTC marks.

---

## Why the market might be wrong

The market prices MSTR as a levered call on bitcoin with an embedded software annuity, not as a normalized software compounder. **[Inference]** At roughly **$390** per share (placeholder until market refresh), the stock embeds a large premium to filing look-through net asset value (~**$110** per share of GAAP equity at Q1 2026, or ~**$150** per share on a simplified BTC-minus-debt bridge). Mispricing debates are about **mNAV premium persistence**, dilution from the capital-markets flywheel, and whether spot bitcoin ETFs arbitrage away the equity wrapper. There is no dated equity yield curve; the predictive attribute is **levered digital-asset accumulation** rather than a passive royalty trust.

---

## Executive summary

Strategy is an **optionality / levered** name: a small but cash-generative analytics business plus a massive, continuously growing bitcoin stack financed by convertibles and preferreds. Marvin’s **software-only** owner-cash path (~**$0.26–$0.65** per share normalized) cannot justify the equity price; the investment case lives in the **look-through BTC payoff** and capital-structure path. Base **7-year annual return at today’s price** on the look-through yield curve is **pending mechanical refresh** (placeholder scenarios target mid-single-digit base / low-teens bull). **Dhando** is **partial** (hard asset, unbounded dilution and mark volatility). **Moat** on software is **unproven** at portfolio weight. We propose **watch** until mNAV, dilution, and BTC spot inputs are refreshed and sized.

## Risks & inversion

**Primary risk:** **mNAV compression** combined with **dilution** from convertibles and perpetual preferred issuance while BTC marks reverse.

- **Mark volatility:** Q1 2026 unrealized loss **$14.5B** on one quarter’s BTC move (10-Q).  
- **Liquidity / refinancing:** **$8.2B+** debt maturities concentrated years 2–3 (10-Q debt table).  
- **Regulatory / tax:** CAMT on unrealized gains, digital-asset classification risk (10-K risk factors).  
- **ETF arbitrage:** Spot BTC ETPs may cap equity-wrapper premium (10-K).

**Inversion:** If we are wrong, MSTR fairly prices a successful BTC accumulation engine and common equity still outperforms direct BTC due to tax-efficient leverage and premium persistence.

**Lens failure mode (levered):** Treating GAAP EPS or a single-point BTC price as predictable; ignoring preferred/conversion dilution; using GAAP book as a floor when the thesis is premium-to-NAV.
