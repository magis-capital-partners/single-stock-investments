Explanatory Note

This Amendment No. 1 to Schedule 13D (“Amendment No. 1”) amends and supplements the Schedule 13D originally filed with the United States Securities and Exchange Commission (the “SEC”) on February 22, 2024 (as amended to date, the “Schedule 13D”), relating to the shares of common stock, par value $0.0001 per share (the “Common Stock”), of Sable Offshore Corp., a Delaware corporation (the “Issuer”). Capitalized terms used herein without definition shall have the meanings set forth in the Schedule 13D.

This Amendment No. 1 is being filed as a result of the appointment of the Sarofim Trust Co., which the Reporting Person exercises investment control over, as trustee of the Christopher B. Sarofim 2017 Gift Trust on June 1, 2024.
