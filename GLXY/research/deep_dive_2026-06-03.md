## What this business is

Galaxy Digital Inc. is a global digital-asset financial services and infrastructure company listed on Nasdaq (GLXY). **[Fact]** It serves institutional allocators, hedge funds, family offices, and qualified individuals through trading, investment banking, asset management, staking, tokenization, and custody technology. **[Fact]** Reportable segments are **Digital Assets**, **Data Centers** (Helios HPC campus in Texas), and **Treasury and Corporate** (proprietary digital-asset and venture investments). **[Fact]** The company went public via reorganization in 2025; FY2025 was its first full year as a U.S. registrant. **[Fact]** As of December 31, 2025, the platform had about **$12.3 billion** in assets and **190.7 million** Class A shares outstanding.

---

## Why the market might be wrong

The stock may trade like a levered crypto proxy while understating the recurring fee and infrastructure franchise. **[Inference]** FY2025 **Adjusted EBITDA was only $33.7 million** (down from **$715.4 million** in 2024) because treasury marks and trading volatility dominated GAAP earnings, not because fee lines collapsed: **fees rose 54%** to **$159 million** and blockchain rewards grew to **$220 million**. **[Inference]** The **Helios** data-center pivot (CoreWeave lease, **133 MW** of critical IT load targeted by mid-2026) is priced as optionality with **zero Data Center segment revenue** in FY2025. **[Inference]** Complexity (dual share classes, digital-asset balance sheet, mark-to-market earnings) keeps generalist investors away, so the market may overweight trailing losses and underweight institutional wallet share.

---

## Executive summary

Galaxy is a multi-sided digital-asset platform transitioning from crypto-cycle earnings to a mix of institutional fees, staking infrastructure, and Texas HPC hosting. FY2025 net loss was **$482 million** on volatile marks; Q1 2026 remained loss-making (**$92 million** net loss, **$(187.5) million** Adjusted EBITDA). At **$28.48** the equity trades near **$5.4 billion** Class A market cap on **$3.0 billion** total equity.

Dhando is **partial** (equity cushion, not clean operating floor). Stance: **watch**.

## Risks & inversion

**Primary risk:** Digital-asset price and volume collapse simultaneously hits trading profits, AUM fees, and **$3.7 billion** balance-sheet marks.

- **Regulatory:** SEC or other agencies classify key activities or tokens as securities, restricting U.S. operations.
- **HPC execution:** Helios retrofit delays or CoreWeave concentration leave Data Centers segment sub-scale.
- **Governance / dilution:** Dual-class structure and shelf registrations (S-3ASR May 2026) can dilute minorities if equity is issued in a downturn.

**Lens failure mode (levered):** Treating Galaxy as a stable compounder while sizing off peak **2024** Adjusted EBITDA (**$715M**) would overstate owner cash; treating it as equity stub without fee franchise would understate the platform.

**Inversion:** Permanent loss of institutional wallet share, Helios contract default, or equity issuance below book would invalidate watch-for-entry.
