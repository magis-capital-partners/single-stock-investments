## What this business is

Gold Reserve Ltd. is a Bermuda company (continued from Alberta in September 2024) that no longer operates a mine. **[Fact]** Its economic identity is a **judgment-and-claim vehicle**: collect unpaid amounts under a 2014 ICSID award and 2016 Venezuela settlement, participate as a creditor/bidder in the Delaware forced sale of PDV Holding (CITGO’s parent stack), and prosecute a 2025 arbitration over revoked Siembra Minera mining rights. **[Fact]** Shares trade as **GDRZF** (OTCQX) and **GRZ.V** (TSXV). **[Fact]** The company deregistered from the SEC on 30 January 2025, so U.S. EDGAR is no longer the primary reporting channel. **[Fact]**

## Executive summary

Gold Reserve is not an operating gold miner. Owners own a stack of Venezuela-related legal claims plus corporate cash, after CVR and bonus-plan leakage. Local folders hold Delaware sale orders, a Lisbon recognition decision, an Arb II request for arbitration, a 2018 NI 43-101, and a PFIC notice, but **not** the latest SEDAR financial statements. At about **$4.56** per share, the market capitalizes substantial recovery expectations versus a soft cash floor near **$0.70** per share. Base case annual return is **11.3% per year** on a **$7** per share risked payoff in about four years. That is below our usual fifteen percent target after process, OFAC, ranking, and dilution risk. Dhando is **partial** (cash helps; binary legal outcomes dominate). Stance: **watch** until audited cash/shares and the Amber closing path are verified on SEDAR and court dockets.
