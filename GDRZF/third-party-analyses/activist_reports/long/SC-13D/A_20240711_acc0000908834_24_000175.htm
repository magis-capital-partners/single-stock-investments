Capitalized terms used without definition in this Amendment have the meanings ascribed thereto in the Prior Schedule 13D.

Preliminary Note

Capitalized terms used in this Preliminary Note without definition have the meanings ascribed to them elsewhere in this Schedule 13D.

As used herein, the term “July 2024 Offering” refers to the Company’s private placement which, as disclosed by the Company in its Form 6-K filed with the SEC on July 3, 2024, closed on such date and entailed the issuance and sale by the Company of an aggregate 8,780,488 Shares.
