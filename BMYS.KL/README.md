## Business

Bursa Malaysia is the national stock exchange and multi-asset marketplace operator for Malaysia. Revenue comes from securities and derivatives trading fees, listing and depository services, Islamic market (Bursa Suq Al-Sila'), market data, and exchange holding activities. The group also operates Bursa Carbon Exchange (BCX) and digital investor platforms (MyBURSA).
