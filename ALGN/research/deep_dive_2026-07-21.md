## What this business is
Align Technology manufactures and sells Invisalign clear aligners and the iTero intraoral scanner ecosystem to orthodontists and general dentists worldwide. **[Fact]** FY2025 net revenue was **$3.86 billion**; Clear Aligner net revenues were **$3.25 billion** (~84% of consolidated), and Systems and Services were **$790 million** (~20%). **[Fact]** FY2025 operating cash flow was **$786 million** minus capital spending of **$178 million**, on **76.6 million** diluted shares. **[Fact]** The balance sheet held **$1.04 billion** cash with **no long-term debt** and an undrawn **$300 million** revolving credit facility. **[Inference]** Align earns per-case fees from doctor-directed orthodontic treatment plus scanner lease, software, and services revenue that lock doctors into the digital workflow.

---

## Why the market might be wrong
The stock trades near component-sum fair value while the market may overweight **post-pandemic volume normalization** and underweight **scanner ecosystem retention**. **[Fact]** Q1 FY2026 revenue grew **6.2%** year over year to **$1.04 billion** with diluted EPS **$1.57** versus **$1.27** prior year; operating cash flow improved to **$151 million** from **$53 million**. **[Inference]** Conversely, the market may underprice **DTC competition and average selling price pressure** on adult cases. **[Fact]** FY2025 research and development was **$347 million** and share-based compensation **$154 million**, reflecting continued product investment amid litigation (antitrust, Straumann, Angelalign). **[Inference]** No dated HK predictive attribute exists; the edge is **franchise quality at fair price**, not a catalyst with a known payoff date.

---

## Executive summary
Align is a clear-aligner compounder with a dominant doctor channel, strong cash conversion, and a net-cash balance sheet. FY2025 free cash flow per share is about **$7.94**; at **~$175** (July 2026), total synthesis implies **10.37%** per year over 7 years on filing-based owner cash. Moat is **stable** (scanner workflow, brand, doctor training) but not widening; **partial** dhando because net cash (~**$11** per share after operating minimum) caps the bear case while ASP and teen-volume cyclicality limit upside. Marvin proposes **watch** on math; **[HUMAN REVIEW]** whether Q1 FY2026 volume recovery persists.
