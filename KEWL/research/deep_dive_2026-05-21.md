## Executive summary

Keweenaw is a **micro-cap mineral royalty and land company** (~1.1M shares outstanding) holding **1M+ net mineral acres** in Michigan/Wisconsin, with primary near-term economics tied to **Highland Copper's Copperwood project** leases (~$350K/year lease income in FY2024). The 2024 acquisition of **667K gross mineral acres** for ~$1.02M (~$1.52/acre) materially expanded optionality.

**One-line view:** Optionality on Michigan copper/minerals with a patient capital structure — **not** a dhando compounder today; thesis is **asset value + eventual royalty streams**, offset by steady-state operating burn and single-tenant concentration.

## Munger lenses

### Circle of competence — specialized

Understandable business model (mineral lessor), but **reserve geology, Highland Copper counterparty risk, and Michigan mining politics** require domain expertise or partner trust.

### Moat

- **Asset uniqueness:** Dominant mineral estate in Upper Peninsula — hard to replicate
- **No pricing power** until production royalties activate
- **Switching costs** low for lessees until mine built

### Inversion — what would kill the thesis?

1. **Copperwood never reaches production** — lease income only; Highland financial distress
2. **Environmental / political block** on Michigan mining (management acknowledges activist risk)
3. **Burn rate exceeds treasury** before royalty cash flows
4. **Illiquid OTC** — no exit, wide spreads
5. **Book value of mineral rights** overstated vs realizable value

## Copperwood / Highland Copper watchlist

**Facts** (FY2024 annual letter and MD&A):

- Four mineral leases to Highland Copper; **$349,700** lease income in 2024 with escalating rents
- Leases convert to **royalty-based** arrangement upon mine production
- Highland pursuing engineering/metallurgical studies; **$50M Michigan infrastructure grant** application cited as slow but "in play"
- Keweenaw owns ~**80%** of Copperwood proven/probable reserves footprint after Sage acquisition

**Inference** — near-term stock driver is **lease income + exploration partnering** on the 667K-acre 2024 package; long-term driver is **Copperwood production royalties** (timing uncertain).
