## What this business is

XP Inc. is Brazil's leading independent investment platform. **[Fact]** The company connects retail and institutional clients to mutual funds, fixed income, equities, insurance, credit cards, and lending through a network of independent financial advisors and direct digital channels. **[Fact]** FY2025 client assets reached **R$1,491 billion** with **4.7 million** active clients, roughly **12%** of an estimated **R$8 trillion** addressable market. **[Fact]** Revenue comes from advisory and distribution fees on client assets, brokerage commissions, securities placement, and net income on financial instruments the firm intermediates. XP files as a Brazilian broker-dealer and related subsidiaries; it is not a stock exchange.

## Why the market might be wrong

**[Inference]** The ADR trades near **8–9 times** adjusted earnings despite mid-teens earnings growth and a dominant share position in Brazilian retail wealth. **[Inference]** Investors may lump XP with generic Latin American financials or past governance controversies, underweighting the recurring fee stream tied to **R$1.5 trillion** of client assets confirmed in the Apr 2026 FY2025 20-F. **[Inference]** Headline total revenue and income (**R$18.4 billion**, +8% versus last year) mixes high-margin service fees with mark-to-market financial instrument income, obscuring the steadier retail distribution engine. Q1 2026 capital return headlines (buyback, dividend) and a planned CFO transition remain news-only; they may tighten the earnings-yield floor if executed, but we do not move them into base owner cash without a filed 6-K.
