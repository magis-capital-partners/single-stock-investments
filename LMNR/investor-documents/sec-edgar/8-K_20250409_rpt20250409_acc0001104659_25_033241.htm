Item 7.01Regulation FD Disclosure

 

On April 9 2025, Limoneira Company, a Delaware corporation (the “Company”), will hold its annual stockholders’ meeting at 10:00 a.m., Pacific Time, at the Museum of Ventura County Agriculture Museum, 926 Railroad Avenue, Santa Paula, California 93060. The Company intends to use the presentation (the “2025 Annual Meeting Presentation”) attached to this report at that meeting. The 2025 Annual Meeting Presentation is furnished as Exhibit 99.1 to this report and is incorporated by reference into this Item 7.01.

 

The information in this Item 7.01, including Exhibit 99.1 attached hereto, is being furnished and shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (“Exchange Act”), or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that Section and shall not be incorporated by reference into any registration statement or other document pursuant to the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act, except as otherwise expressly stated in such filing.
