The Offer is being made pursuant to an offer to purchase (the "Offer to Purchase"), dated December 2, 2004, which is attached hereto as Exhibit (a)(2)(ii). The Offer is an "odd-lot tender offer" pursuant to Rule 13e-4(h)(5) under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the "Exchange Act"). In addition, because the Offer has a reasonable likelihood or a purpose of causing the Class A Common Stock to be held of record by fewer than 300 persons, the Offer is a "Rule 13e-3 transaction" as defined in Rule 13e-3(a)(3) under the Exchange Act.
