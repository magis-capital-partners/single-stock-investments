**Last updated:** 2026-06-12

## Company

Star Holdings is the March 2023 spin-off of iStar Inc.'s legacy non-ground-lease assets. The portfolio includes loans, operating properties, land (Asbury Park, Magnolia Green), and ~13.5M Safehold Inc. (SAFE) shares. Externally managed by a Safehold subsidiary; strategy is asset monetization and share repurchases.

**IR:** https://www.starholdingsco.com · investors@starholdingsco.com
