## What this business is
Nebius Group is a Netherlands-domiciled AI cloud infrastructure company, spun off from Yandex, that builds and operates GPU datacenters for AI training and inference. **[Fact]** FY2025 continuing operations generated early-stage revenue with an operating loss of **$285.7 million** (`investor-documents/sec-edgar/20-F_20260430_rpt20251231_acc0001104659_26_052948.htm`). **[Fact]** Deferred revenue totaled approximately **$1.32 billion** (current **$16.3 million** plus noncurrent **$1,302 million**), reflecting contracted GPU capacity not yet recognized. **[Fact]** Cash was **$2.43 billion** and long-term debt **$4.10 billion** at December 31, 2025. **[Fact]** Diluted weighted-average shares were **370.8 million**. **[Fact]** Discontinued Yandex operations produced large GAAP net income; Lawrence math uses continuing AI cloud only.

---

## Why the market might be wrong
The market may capitalize **$1.32 billion** deferred revenue as near-term owner cash while GPU build-out still burns capital. **[Inference]** FY2025 continuing operating loss on small revenue implies spot price embeds rapid backlog conversion. **[Inference]** Prior-year capital spending of **$807 million** (continuing) shows the capex cycle; trough-year stress could drive **-$3.45** per share free cash flow before normalization. **[Inference]** AI inflection bull case (**28.8%** per year in overlay) requires **$0.85** per share starting cash with 45% growth, not in Lawrence base.
