# LandBridge Company LLC (LB)

**Ticker:** LB | **Exchange:** NYSE | **Market:** US  
**CIK:** 1995807  
**Last updated:** 2026-06-02

## Company

LandBridge owns and manages Permian Basin (Delaware) surface acreage, collecting fee-based revenue from easements, surface use royalties, resource sales/royalties, and oil and gas royalties. Formed by Five Point Infrastructure; related to WaterBridge (WBI) ecosystem.
