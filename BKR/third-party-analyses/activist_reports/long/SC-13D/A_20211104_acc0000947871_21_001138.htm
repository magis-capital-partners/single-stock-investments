Item 4. Purpose of the Transaction.

Item 4 of the Amended Schedule 13D is hereby supplemented by adding the following paragraph:

“On November 3, 2021, the Stockholder sold 47,380,978 shares of Class A Common Stock in connection with the settlement of a Forward Transaction. As a result, the 20% Trigger Date occurred and GE’s right to designate a director for nomination to the board of directors of the Issuer (the “BKR Board”) terminated. The Conflicts Committee waived the obligation of the GE designee serving on the BKR Board to deliver his resignation from the BKR Board for consideration by the Conflicts Committee and such designee (John Rice) will therefore continue as a member of the BKR Board (but not as a designee of GE).”

Item 5. Interest in Securities of the Issuer.

Section (a) of Item 5 of the Amended Schedule 13D is hereby amended and restated in its entirety as follows:

“(a) Based on the most recent information available, the aggregate number and percentage of the Class A Common Stock (the securities identified pursuant to Item 1 of this Amendment) that are beneficially owned by each of the Reporting Persons as of the date of the Amendment is set forth in boxes (11) and (13) of the cover pages to this Amendment for each of the Reporting Persons, and such information is incorporated herein by reference. The percentages reported herein are calculated based on the Issuer’s information as of October 15, 2021 (which includes the exchange of Paired Interests for shares of Class A Common Stock and the retirement of shares of Class B Common Stock as described in the Exchange Agreement) and the exchanges of (i) 35,300,718 Paired Interests on August 3, 2021, (ii) 7,882,621 Paired Interests on October 5, 2021 and (iii) 4,197,639 Paired Interests on October 13, 2021, resulting in 166,645,919 shares of Class B Common Stock and 869,968,050 shares of Class A Common Stock outstanding and, assuming the exchange of all Paired Interests into Class A Common Stock, a total of 1,036,613,969 shares of Class A Common Stock on a fully exchanged basis.”

 

Section (c) of Item 5 of the Amended Schedule 13D is hereby amended and restated in its entirety as follows:

“(c) The information set forth, or incorporated by reference in, Item 4 in this Schedule 13D is hereby incorporated by reference.”
