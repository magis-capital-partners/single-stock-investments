## What this business is
AXT Inc. (NASDAQ: AXTI) manufactures specialty compound semiconductor wafer substrates and related raw materials. **[Fact]** The company produces indium phosphide (InP), gallium arsenide (GaAs), and germanium (Ge) wafers used when silicon cannot meet frequency or optical performance requirements. **[Fact]** Two consolidated subsidiaries also produce gallium raw materials (85.5% owned). **[Fact]** Manufacturing is concentrated in China through subsidiaries and joint ventures, with corporate headquarters in Fremont, California. **[Fact]** FY2025 revenue was **$88.3 million**; the company reported a **$21.3 million** net loss and **negative** operating cash flow. **[Inference]** Cash to owners today is not positive; the investment case rests on substrate cycle recovery, AI-driven InP demand, and optional value from subsidiary Tongmei's capital-markets path.

---

## Why the market might be wrong
The market may be pricing **AI optical connectivity optionality** (InP for high-speed data transmission) while underweighting **China manufacturing and export-permit risk**, **customer concentration** (top five customers were **67%** of FY2025 revenue), **share dilution** (shares outstanding rose from roughly **43.5 million** to **65.4 million** between Q1 2025 and May 2026), and **Tongmei private-equity redemption overhang** after the July 2026 withdrawal of the STAR Market listing application. **[Inference]** Q1 2026 revenue of **$26.9 million** (+39% year over year) and gross margin near **30%** suggest operating inflection, but reported owner cash is still negative and mid-cycle normalization requires evidence that new supply contracts convert to sustained free cash flow, not just capacity spending at Tongmei.

---

## Executive summary
AXT is a cyclical specialty-substrate supplier levered to InP demand from datacenter and AI optical interconnect markets. FY2025 was a loss year (gross margin **12.7%**, operating cash flow **-$12.8 million**), but Q1 2026 shows revenue acceleration and margin recovery. Recent Coherent and Casela long-term InP supply agreements validate end-market pull. The balance sheet carries **$57.9 million** in cash and restricted cash at March 31, 2026, partially offset by Tongmei PE redemption rights of roughly **$49 million** after the STAR Market IPO withdrawal. At **$45.86** (Yahoo close 2026-07-17), the weighted synthesis base case yields **-9.92%** per year over seven years on **$0.12 per share** mid-cycle normalized owner cash (not the negative spot filing number). Lawrence scenario-only return is **-32.8%**. The stock prices substantial AI/InP optionality that normalized cash flow does not support at this multiple. Moat is **unproven**; dhando is **partial**. Proposed stance: **watch**.

- **Customer concentration:** Top five customers **67%** of FY2025 revenue; loss of one major customer would materially impair utilization.
