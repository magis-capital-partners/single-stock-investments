# Bolsas y Mercados Argentinos S.A. (BYMA)

**Market:** Argentina (BYMA / BCBA)  
**Ticker:** BYMA  
**Exchange listing:** Bolsa de Comercio de Buenos Aires

## Company

BYMA operates Argentina's integrated market infrastructure: the stock exchange (trading, clearing, central counterparty), central securities depository Caja de Valores S.A. (99.97% owned), and education subsidiary IAMC. Created under Capital Markets Law 26.831; successor to Mercado de Valores de Buenos Aires.
