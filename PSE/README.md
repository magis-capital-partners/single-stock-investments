## Company

The Philippine Stock Exchange, Inc. operates the national equity market and related post-trade infrastructure (listing, trading, clearing, depository, market data). Subsidiaries include Philippine Dealing System Holdings Corp. (PDSHC), Philippine Depository & Trust Corp. (PDTC), and Philippine Dealing & Exchange Corp. (PDEx).
