## What this business is
Teqnion AB (TEQ.ST, Nasdaq First North) is a Swedish industrial serial acquirer. **[Fact]** The parent owned **38** operating subsidiaries at year-end 2025 (30 prior year) across niche industrial niches in Nordics and the UK. **[Fact]** FY2025 net sales were **1,800.0 MSEK** (+15%); organic net sales **-5%** after deliberate exits of unprofitable units. **[Fact]** FY2025 EBITA was **203.1 MSEK** (+36%) at an **11.3%** margin; EPS **5.74 SEK**. **[Fact]** Operating cash flow was **184.6 MSEK** (+92%); the board proposes **no dividend** for FY2025. Cash returns are modest; the model is reinvestment plus EPS growth.

## Why the market might be wrong
Reported **organic net sales -5%** may anchor sentiment while margin and cash-flow inflection are under-discounted. **[Inference]** FY2025 EBITA margin rose from **9.6%** to **11.3%** and Q1 2026 EBITA margin hit a record **14.3%** (prior interim, inventory tier), suggesting pruning is working. There is no dated HK payoff; mispricing, if any, is franchise quality at a full multiple (~**15.7×** FY2025 FCF/share at **~158 SEK**).

## Executive summary
Teqnion is a proven decentralized serial acquirer with a strengthening margin story. FY2025 net sales rose **15%** on nine bolt-on deals while organic sales fell **5%** after deliberate exits of weak units. EBITA grew **36%** to **203.1 MSEK** at an **11.3%** margin, and operating cash flow jumped **92%** to **184.6 MSEK**. Free cash flow excluding acquisitions was **173.1 MSEK**, or **10.08 SEK** per share, bridged from Not 27 in the full-tier annual report. Goodwill impairment of **73 MSEK** in Q3 2025 and contingent consideration of **195.6 MSEK** remind us that deal quality matters. At **~165 SEK**, proof-first component value sums to roughly **142 SEK** per share while total synthesis implies about **17.3%** per year over seven years, below our **15%** accumulate gate at this price. Moat durability is stable at the portfolio level; dhando is partial given M&A and earnout risk. We propose **watch** unless price falls toward **~130 SEK** for a cleaner Lawrence return.
