## What this business is
QuidelOrtho is a post-merger diagnostics company (Quidel plus Ortho Clinical Diagnostics, 2022). **[Fact]** It sells lab instruments, point-of-care tests, molecular assays, and donor screening consumables to hospitals, labs, and blood centers. **[Fact]** Revenue is largely recurring consumables on installed bases, not toll-collector exchange economics. **[Fact]** FY2025 revenue was **$2,730.2M** (-1.9% YoY per `10-K_20260219...htm`); stockholders' equity **$1,920.5M** (down from $2,984.5M). Q1 FY2026 revenue was **$619.8M** (-10.5% YoY vs $692.8M prior-year quarter, same 10-Q). FY2026 guidance: revenue **$2.70 to $2.75B**; adjusted EBITDA **$615 to $630M** (company deck).

---

## Why the market might be wrong
A **transitory problem** may apply: merger integration noise, respiratory normalization, and China headwinds depress the top line while self-help margin expansion (+240 bps FY2025) is real. **[Inference]** The stock near **~$12.18** prices a broken merger, but owner cash is uncertain until revenue stabilizes. McIntyre Partnerships (May 2026 letter) stresses the same issues but normalizes to **~$4 FCF/sh by 2028** and **~$60** (15×). We **triangulate** rather than pick one side (see Blended estimate).
