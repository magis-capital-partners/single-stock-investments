Item 8.01. Other Events.

 

On March 11, 2026, XTI Drones, LLC, a wholly-owned subsidiary of XTI Aerospace, Inc. (the “Company”), filed a Certificate of Formation with the Secretary of State of Texas to form XTI Drones - Defense, LLC, a direct subsidiary of XTI Drones, LLC and an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of the Company. The formation of XTI Drones - Defense, LLC is intended to support and further the Company’s publicly disclosed corporate strategy, which includes exploring opportunities in the Vertical Economy™.
