European airlines and the Strait of Hormuz.
Europe is exposed to a jet-fuel shortage tied to disruption at the Strait of Hormuz.
Airlines are only partially hedged and are not priced for a lasting disruption.
In previous jet-fuel shortages, spreads regularly widened to ~700bp.
If disruption to oil shipments persists, airlines are among the most affected sectors.
Even on a more moderate widening, we see upside above 100%.
