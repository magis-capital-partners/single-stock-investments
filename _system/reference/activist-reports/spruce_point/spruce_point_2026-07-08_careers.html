Working For Spruce Point

Spruce Point Capital Management values intellectual curiosity and honesty, critical thinking, intense and rigorous financial analysis, and accountability for results. Spruce Point's strong company culture emphasizes teamwork and absolute returns for our investors. Spruce Point’s leadership team believes that attracting and retaining talent is one of the firm’s biggest continued drivers of success.

We are currently seeking Hedge Fund Analysts who have a passion for researching companies and industries and are capable of working independently to achieve superior results.

Candidates should possess the following:

  • Passion for market research and desire to understand what drives industry and company performance
  • Demonstrated experience in an industry, or group of industries
  • Superior financial modeling and valuation skills
  • Basic understanding of accounting and curiousity to learn more
  • Ability to write and produce investment memorandums and defend investment recommendations
  • Superior academic achievement and track record of success
  • 2–5 years experience in financial services
  • B.S./B.A/M.S. in any of the following (mathematics, statistics, engineering, economics, finance)
